AgriLand and Nugent couple up for ‘Ploughing 2019’A Co. Tyrone-based trailer manufacturer has 'coupled up' with AgriLand for 'Ploughing 2019' to help in the mammoth task of getting AgriLand's equipment on site for the three-day event.
Nugent Trailers has came on board providing not one but two trailers, including this tri-axle trailer (pictured above).
These trailers will be used at major farming events such this year's 'Ploughing' in Fenagh, Co. Carlow.
Nugent Trailers at 'Ploughing 2019'

Nugent Engineering is a well-known manufacturer throughout the island of Ireland.
Its product range includes:

Bale handling equipment;
Shear grabs;
Tine grabs;
Multi purpose trailers;
Power link boxes;
Yard scrapers;
Toppers;
