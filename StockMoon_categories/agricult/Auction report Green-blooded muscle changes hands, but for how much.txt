Auction report Green-blooded muscle changes hands, but for how muchAn on-site auction of tractors and farm machinery took place at Washpit Farm, Grimston, King's Lynn (England), on Wednesday, February 20.
The sale involved some noteworthy lots, including a number of high-horsepower John Deere and Deutz-Fahr tractors, as well as a myriad of other items.
The auction was conducted by Cruso & Wilkin.

This reports focuses on the newer, higher-value tractors that were up for grabs. Look out for accompanying reports, in which we detail what other lots sold for.
