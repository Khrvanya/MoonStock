Beyond Meat burgers to be sold at some Costco stores1 Min ReadCHICAGO, Dec 5 (Reuters) - Beyond Meat Inc said on Thursday that its plant-based burgers will be sold at some Costco Wholesale Corp stores in the United States. The company said its popular Beyond Burgers will be sold in states including Texas, Florida and New York for $14.99 per eight-patty pack.
