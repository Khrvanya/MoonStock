China must achieve pork output recovery, stabilise supply - vice premier1 Min ReadBEIJING, Dec 1 (Reuters) - China's vice premier said the country must resolutely achieve the target of pig production recovery, and stabilise pork supply for the upcoming holidays, the official Xinhua News Agency reported. China must ensure stable pork supply in key periods in early 2020, including the Chinese Lunar New Year in January and the annual gathering of the National People's Congress in March, said Hu Chunhua during comments at a meeting on animal husbandry on Saturday.
