China says Jan-Feb trade to take sharp hit from virus outbreak1 Min ReadBEIJING, Feb 21 (Reuters) - China's January and February exports and imports will be hit by  the coronavirus outbreak that has severely disrupted the world's second-largest economy, the country's commerce ministry said on Friday. Ministry officials said during a press briefing that China expects foreign companies in most places to resume production by end-Febuary, but added there will be a more pronounced impact on foreign direct investment into China in February and March.
