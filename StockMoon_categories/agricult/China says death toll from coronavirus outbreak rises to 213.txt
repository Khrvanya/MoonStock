China says death toll from coronavirus outbreak rises to 2131 Min ReadBEIJING, Jan 31 (Reuters) - China's National Health Commission (NHC) said on Friday the death toll from the coronavirus outbreak in the country rose to 213 as of end-Thursday, with another 1,982 new cases confirmed. The total number of confirmed coronavirus cases in China reached 9,692 as of end-Thursday, NHC said in a statement.
