China says economy faces increasing negative impact from virus outbreak1 Min ReadBEIJING, Feb 3 (Reuters) - China's economy is facing increasing negative impact from the virus outbreak, especially on consumption, a vice chairman of the country's state planner said on Monday. Lian Weiliang of China's National Development and Reform Commission said during a press briefing the impact from the outbreak will be for the short term and that China is fully capable of minimising the economic impact from the outbreak.
