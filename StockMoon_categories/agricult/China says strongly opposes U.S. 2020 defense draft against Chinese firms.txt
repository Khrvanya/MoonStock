China says strongly opposes U.S. 2020 defense draft against Chinese firms1 Min ReadBEIJING (Reuters) - China strongly opposes the 2020 U.S. defense draft against Chinese firms and urges Washington to go past its political bias to create favorable conditions for Chinese companies, China's commerce ministry said on Thursday. China will closely monitor the situation and defend the interests of Chinese firms, Gao Feng, spokesman at the ministry, said at a regular media briefing.
