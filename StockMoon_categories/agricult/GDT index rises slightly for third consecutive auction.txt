GDT index rises slightly for third consecutive auctionThe latest Global Dairy Trade (GDT) auction has seen the international index climb marginally for a third successive time.
Held earlier today, Tuesday, October 15, the GDT's event 246 concluded with the GDT Price Index up 0.5%.

A total of 38,712MT of product was sold on the day.
