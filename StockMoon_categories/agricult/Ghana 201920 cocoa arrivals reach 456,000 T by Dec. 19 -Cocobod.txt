Ghana 201920 cocoa arrivals reach 456,000 T by Dec. 19 -Cocobod1 Min ReadABIDJAN, Jan 6 (Reuters) - Ghana's graded and sealed (G&S) cocoa arrivals stood at 456,000 tonnes as of Dec. 19, unchanged from last year, figures from marketing board Cocobod showed on Monday. The 2019 harvest started on Oct. 1. G&S is cocoa that has been quality checked and sealed in bags by Cocobod and is ready to be shipped. Cocoa production in Ghana is expected to reach 850,000 tonnes this season, Cocobod has forecast.
