Ivory Coast 201920 cocoa port arrivals seen at 694,000 T by Dec. 1 - exporters1 Min ReadABIDJAN, Dec 2 (Reuters) - Cocoa arrivals at ports in top grower Ivory Coast reached 694,000 tonnes between Oct. 1 and Dec. 1, exporters estimated on Monday, down about 2% from 708,000 tonnes over the same period last season. About 40,000 tonnes of beans were delivered to Abidjan port and 47,000 tonnes to San Pedro between Nov. 25 and Dec. 1 for a total of 87,000 tonnes, up from 83,000 tonnes during the same week last season.
