Russia's VTB says plans grain holding stake sale - Ifax1 Min ReadMOSCOW, Dec 20 (Reuters) - Russia's second largest lender VTB Bank said it may sell a stake in its grain holding company to a private investor, the Interfax news agency on Friday cited VTB First Deputy CEO Yuri Soloviev. Soloviev said VTB plans to keep a controlling stake in the grain holding, adding that interest in the sale has been high, including from foreign investors.
