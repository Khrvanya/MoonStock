Seed oilseed rape contracts could add to your rotationSeedtech is offering spring oilseed rape seed contracts for the 2019 season. According to Seedtech, growers can expect to make twice the margin of oilseed rape grown for the commodity market.
The contracts comprise of:

A fixed oilseed payment of EUR450/t at 9% moisture content;
A land payment of EUR202/ac;
