Senate Democratic leader Schumer 'waiting to see the writing' on USMCA1 Min ReadWASHINGTON, Dec 10 (Reuters) - Senate Democratic leader Chuck Schumer said on Tuesday he would wait to see the written form of the U.S. Mexico Canada trade pact before making a determination on it. "I'm waiting to see the writing," he said after top officials from Canada, Mexico and the United States signed a fresh overhaul of their quarter-century-old regional trade pact.
