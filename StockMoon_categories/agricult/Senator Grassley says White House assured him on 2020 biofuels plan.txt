Senator Grassley says White House assured him on 2020 biofuels plan1 Min ReadNEW YORK, Dec 10 (Reuters) - U.S. Senator Chuck Grassley said on Tuesday he has been assured by the White House Office of Management and Budget that 2020 biofuel blending mandates will be finalized in accordance with a September agreement between the administration and biofuel and corn producers. The Iowa Republican said in a call with reporters that he had been assured about the mandates in a recent conversation with OMB Acting Director Russell Vought, and had also held recent conversations on the issue with President Donald Trump's economic advisor Larry Kudlow.
