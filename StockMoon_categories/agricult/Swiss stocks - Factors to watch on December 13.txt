Swiss stocks - Factors to watch on December 132 Min ReadBERLIN/ZURICH, Dec 13 (Reuters) - The Swiss blue-chip SMI was seen opening 0.8% higher at 10,532 points on Friday, according to premarket indications by bank Julius Baer .
