Top U.S. officials heading to Mexico in press to finalize USMCA -reports1 Min ReadWASHINGTON, Dec 9 (Reuters) - U.S. Trade Representative Robert Lighthizer and White House adviser Jared Kushner will head to Mexico on Tuesday as part of a push to finalize the U.S.-Mexico-Canada (USMCA) trade pact, which has yet to be ratified by the U.S. Congress, according to media reports.
