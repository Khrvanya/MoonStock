Ukraine sunflower oil exports jump 59% so far in 201920 - APK-Inform1 Min ReadKIEV, Feb 10 (Reuters) - Ukraine, the world's top exporter of sunflower oil, increased its seaborne exports of the commodity by around 59% so far in the 2019/20 season, preliminary data from the APK-Inform consultancy showed on Monday. Seaport exports of sunflower oil totalled 2.112 million tonnes so far in the 2019/20 season, which runs from August to September, up from 1.329 million tonnes in the same period the previous season, according to the consultancy. The consultancy said exports rose to 77,000 tonnes in the week of Feb. 1-7, from 76,000 tonnes a week earlier. Ukraine exported 6.063 million tonnes of sunflower oil in the previous crop season in 2018-19, according to the Ukrainian sunoil producers' association.
