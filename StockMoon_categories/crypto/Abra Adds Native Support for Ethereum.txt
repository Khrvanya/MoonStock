Abra Adds Native Support for EthereumCrypto service provider Abra has added native Ethereum support to its platform.This development has enabled the cryptocurrency wallet and exchange users to receive, hold, sell, and buy Ethereum directly on the platform. Last October, the firm also launched a cryptocurrency index called BIT10, which tracks the ten largest cryptocurrencies by market capitalization.In addition, to lure the crypto traders, the platform is offering $25 worth Ether to all new customers purchasing at least $5 worth cryptocurrencies on the platform till March 31st.Meanwhile, the blockchain-based platform recently added support to buy fractions of mainstream financial instruments including stocks and exchange-traded funds (ETFs).
