Paxos will sell the stablecoin directly on its platform against a redemption ratio of 1:1 against USD, while Binance will list the digital currency on its exchange platform and traders initially will have the option to trade it against Bitcoin, Binance Coin, and XRP."Paxos is leading the digital trusts space and we are excited to work with them in developing our native stablecoin," Chagpeng "CZ" Zhao, founder and chief executive of Binance, said.
