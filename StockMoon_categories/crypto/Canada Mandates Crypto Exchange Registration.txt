Canada Mandates Crypto Exchange RegistrationCanada has made it mandatory for digital asset exchanges to register with the Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre of Canada (FinTRAC) starting from June 1, 2020.According to the official notice published on June 10, this step has been taken as a part of a major push for anti-money laundering (AML) compliance in the industry.
