However, lenders need to pledge other crypto assets as collateral.The token is developed as an ERC-20 token and can be used to offset transaction fees for crypto-to-crypto and OTC trading on RenrenBit. The token holders can also pledge the tokens as collateral for taking loans on the platform.According to the whitepaper, the 21 million tokens available for public sale consists of only half of the total generated tokens.
