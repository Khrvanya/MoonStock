Circle Closes Acquisition of Crowdfunding Platform SeedInvestBlockchain start-up Circle has completed the acquisition of SeedInvest, a US-based investment crowdfunding platform, in a bid to become a fully SEC-regulated broker-dealer. Deal terms were not revealed.The acquisition puts the Boston-based digital-asset exchange operator on a firmer regulatory footing and positions it to offer a broader range of blockchain-based securities. It also allows the platform to perform all operations in a regulatory compliant manner.Asia Trading Summit - The Leading Investment Event in ChinaFounded in 2012, SeedInvest facilitates the equity-crowdfunding service through its platform, which allows retail investors to browse through different businesses and buy stakes depending on their eligibility.
