Crypto Lending Startup Raises $25M in Andreessen Horowitz-Led RoundA $25 million funding round for crypto lending startup Compound was led by venture capital firm Andreessen Horowitz's a16z, according to a new report from Fortune. Other top investors in the round include Paradigm, Bain Capital Ventures, and Polychain Capital."Compound is a lending protocol that is open to anyone in the world, that disintermediates banks and allows anyone to earn interest on their money," said Andreessen Horowitz general partner Chris Dixon regarding the new partnership.
