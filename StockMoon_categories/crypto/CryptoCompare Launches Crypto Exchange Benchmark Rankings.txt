CryptoCompare Launches Crypto Exchange Benchmark RankingsAmid rising concerns of wash trading, digital asset data provider CryptoCompare on Wednesday launched a crypto exchange benchmark to rank the exchanges.Per the announcement, the benchmark will rank over 100 active global spot exchanges.
