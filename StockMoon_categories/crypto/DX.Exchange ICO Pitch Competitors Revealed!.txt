DX.Exchange ICO Pitch Competitors Revealed!2018 has shown that the demand for crypto projects is still high, with many continuing to set their sights on long-term investments. The winner will be announced by DX.Exchange, which has over 5,000 users, on the Crypto Trading Floor."We are looking for revolutionary projects that solve big problems, improve the crypto ecosystem, and have the potential to disrupt industries using decentralized technology," said Skowronski.
