Fraud Alert Fake Bakkt Website is Seeking FundsFraudsters are targeting vulnerable investors to invest in a fake Bakkt fundraising round, according to a Coindesk report.As per the report, an imposter is sending emails claiming that Bakkt is planning to raise $50 million in a second token sale round. The email was sent from a Gmail address and the contents are written in broken English - a very common theme among scammers.Asia Trading Summit - The Leading Investment Event in ChinaThe email directs the potential victims to bakktplatform.io which ask them to register their names and email addresses.
