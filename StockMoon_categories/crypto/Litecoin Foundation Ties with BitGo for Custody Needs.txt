Litecoin Foundation Ties with BitGo for Custody NeedsAs storing digital assets securely has become very crucial, Litecoin Foundation on Friday announced its partnership with BitGo.With this, the foundation will utilize BitGo's multi-signature wallet for its crypto custodial needs.
