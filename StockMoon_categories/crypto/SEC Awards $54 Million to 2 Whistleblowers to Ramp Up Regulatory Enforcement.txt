SEC Awards $54 Million to 2 Whistleblowers to Ramp Up Regulatory EnforcementThe United States SEC (Securities and Exchange Commission) has paid out $39 million to one whistleblower and $15 million to another-a total of $54 million.The $39 million reward is the second-largest ever granted by the SEC. This rule only applies if the funds collected exceed $1 million.At TechCrunch Disrupt earlier this week, head of the SEC's San Francisco unit Jina Choi revealed that the SEC is intensifying its efforts to investigate private companies.
