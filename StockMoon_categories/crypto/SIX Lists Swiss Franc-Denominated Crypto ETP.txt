SIX Lists Swiss Franc-Denominated Crypto ETPSwiss stock exchange SIX has listed a Swiss-franc denominated exchange-traded product (ETP) on its platform backed by Bitcoin and Ethereum.To launch the ETP, the exchange partnered with fintech firm Amun AG and cryptocurrency custodian Bitcoin Suisse AG and listed the product under the ticker symbol ABBA.
