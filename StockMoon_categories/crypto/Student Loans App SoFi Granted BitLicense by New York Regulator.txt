Student Loans App SoFi Granted BitLicense by New York RegulatorNew York's Department of Financial Services issued its 24th BitLicense today to the online finance and lending start-up SoFi, which authorizes the company to conduct cryptocurrency operations in the state.The state's approval allows SoFi to provide its customers with the ability to buy and sell bitcoin, along with select other virtual coins, for U.S. dollars and vice versa.
