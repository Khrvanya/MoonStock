Any ambiguity under this subsection shall be resolved in favor of the customer," the rules added.Wyoming is one of the few states in the United States to regulate the digital asset industry by introducing new legislation. The state Senate passed a bill this February to recognize cryptocurrencies as moany. In addition to that, the state also passed multiple other bills on tokenization and industry compliance.
