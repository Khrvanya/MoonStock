However, a prototype of tZERO's trading platform was originally launched in April of 2018, but the platform's history extends farther back than last year. The Overstock subsidiary was originally founded all the way back in 2014.The company partnered with New York-based broker-dealer Dinosaur Financial Group to allow accredited investors the ability to trade security tokens through its brokerage accounts.
