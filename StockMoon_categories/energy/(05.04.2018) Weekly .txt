(05.04.2018) Weekly Energy Market Review at a Glance 
05042018

0948
Energy MarketHere's a quick snapshot of the main energy indicators in the last 7 days. 

 
BRENT M+1: 68.12 $/bbl
During the last week, the price rose, driven by the rumours regarding the establishment of a long-term agreement between Saudi Arabia and Russia. At the beginning of this week, the price declined, influenced by the fears of a potential trade war between China and the United States.
 
Outlook (horizon 1 week): A test of the support level of 67.62 $/bbl.
 
GAS TTF Y+1: 17.42 EUR/MWh
The price rose, following the bullish pace of the short-term contracts and influenced by the low levels of the European gas storage sites.
 
Outlook (horizon 1 week): A decline towards the support level of 17.27 EUR/MWh.
 
POWER GE Y+1: 35.53 EUR/MWh
The price rose, pushed by the rise of the gas contracts, the bullish rally of the carbon price and the upward correction of the coal prices.
 
Outlook (horizon 1 week): A rise towards the resistance level of 37.26 EUR/MWh.
