Energy MarketHere's a quick snapshot of the main energy indicators in the last 7 days. 

 
BRENT M+1: 62.45 $/bbl
The oil price rose, driven by the extension of the oil production cuts. At the beginning of this week, the price declined, stimulated by the fears about the rise of the U.S oil production.
 
Outlook: A bearish correction towards the support level of 61.33 $/bbl.
 
GAS TTF Y+1: 18.71 EUR/MWh
The price rose, stimulated by the bullish rally of the spot price.
 
Outlook: A rise towards the resistance level of 19.28 EUR/MWh.
 
POWER GE Y+1: 37.25 EUR/MWh 
The price rose pushed by the upward continuation of the coal prices and the bullish trend of the spot price.
 
Outlook: A test of the support level of 36.86 EUR/MWh followed by a further rise.
