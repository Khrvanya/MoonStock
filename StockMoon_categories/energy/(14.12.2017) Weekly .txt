(14.12.2017) Weekly Energy Market Review at a Glance 
15122017

0940
Energy MarketHere's a quick snapshot of the main energy indicators in the last 7 days. 

 
BRENT M+1: 64.69 $/bbl
The oil price rose, driven by the rise of the Chinese oil imports and the geopolitical tension in the Middle East.
 
Outlook: A test of the resistance level of 66.06 $/bbl.
 
GAS TTF Y+1: 18.62 EUR/MWh
The price rose, stimulated by a technical defect at the Forties Pipeline System in the UK and the explosion at the Austria's main gas pipeline hub.
 
Outlook: A rise towards the resistance level of 19.28 EUR/MWh.
 
POWER GE Y+1: 37.26 EUR/MWh 
The price rose pushed by the upward continuation of the coal prices and the rise of the gas and oil prices.
 
Outlook: A rise towards the resistance level of 38.77 EUR/MWh.
