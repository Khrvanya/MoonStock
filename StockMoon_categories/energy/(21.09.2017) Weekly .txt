(21.09.2017) Weekly Energy Market Review at a Glance 
21092017

0932
Energy MarketHere's a quick snapshot of the main energy indicators in the last 7 days. 
 
BRENT M+1: 55.48 $/bbl
The oil price rose pushed by the IEA's upward revision of the global demand growth and by the easing fears about hurricane disruptions in the U.S.
 
Outlook: A rise towards the resistance line of 57.86 $/bbl.
 
GAS TTF Y+1: 16.97 EUR/MWh
The gas price rose influenced by the bullish European energy complex and by the oil price rally. At the end of the past week the price fell amid a profit - taking movement. Later, the rise continued.
 
Outlook: A test of the resistance level of 17.26 EUR/MWh.
 
POWER GE Y+1: 35.70 EUR/MWh 
The price rose, driven by the bullish continuation of the coal market and by the nuclear concerns in France.
 
Outlook: Another test of the resistance level of 36.86 EUR/MWh, followed by a small bearish correction.
