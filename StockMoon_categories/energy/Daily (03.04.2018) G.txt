Daily (03.04.2018) German spot power price shot 46% up on Monday 
03042018

1516
DailyOil prices plunged on Monday due to a boost in Russian production and reduced concerns over geopolitical situation.Brent crude slumped by $2.63, or around 4%, to settle at $67.64 a barrel. U.S. West Texas Intermediate (WTI) crude futures closed the session down by $1.93, or 3%, at $63.01 a barrel.
 
UK wholesale gas prices and contracts were flat on Monday as no trading activities were undertaken. 
 
European prompt electricity prices jumped on Monday due to lower wind generation. German baseload contract for day-ahead delivery shot 46% up to 34.42 euros a megawatt hour (MWh). French spot price surged by around 7% to settle at 36.87 euros. 
 
Power contracts on the curve have not been traded on Monday. 
 
Easter Holidays left EUAs steady as well on Monday. The carbon contract expiring in 2019 was at 13.36 euros a tonne.
