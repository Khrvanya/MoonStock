Daily (19.02.2018) UK day-ahead gas price hiked by almost 3% on Friday due to colder weather forecasts 
19022018

1136
DailyOil prices soared on Friday backed by a rebound in global equities market and a weaker dollar. Brent crude was up 0.8 percent, to end at $64.84 per barrel. WTI futures increased by 0.6% to $61.68 per barrel.British wholesale gas prices surged on Friday due to lower temperatures forecasts, even though the system was slightly oversupplied. UK day-ahead gas price rose by 2.9% to close at 50.55 p/therm. The Q2 2018 delivery contract traded 1.3% up at 43.00 p/therm.

Electricity wholesale prices varied on Friday. German electricity spot price edged 0.7% higher at 40.69 euros/MWh on forecasts for increased demand because of colder. The French equivalent contract traded 4.6% down at 41.18 euros/MWh, despite a decline in wind and nuclear power generation. 

Forward along the curve, the German year-ahead power price, European benchmark, decreased by 0.1 percent to settle at 34.03 euros/MWh, while the French equivalent price rose 0.5% to 39.33 euros/MWh.

EU carbon prices dropped slightly on Friday after shifting considerably throughout the session. The carbon contract expiring in 2021 slipped by 0.3% to end at 9.91 euros/tonne.
