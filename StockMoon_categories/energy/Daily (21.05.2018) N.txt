Daily (21.05.2018) NBP spot gas price fell by 2.9% on Friday due to warmer weather forecasts 
21052018

1103
DailyOil prices fell on Friday as Saudi Arabia made assurances on supply. Brent crude dropped 0.79 cents, or 1%, to close at $78.51 a barrel. WTI futures edged $0.21, or 0.3% down at $71.28 a barrel. British wholesale gas prices declined on Friday due to warmer weather forecasts which cut demand. UK day-ahead gas price dropped by 1.7 pence, or 2.9% to end at 56.15 p/therm. Meanwhile, June 2018 delivery contract settled 1% lower at 55.46 p/therm.

European wholesale electricity prices tumbled on Friday after a week-long bullish trade which had seen them post a series of all-time peaks due to strong fuels and carbon emissions rights markets. The German day-ahead power price dropped by 7% to end at 36.36 euros/MWh. The French equivalent contract lost 41.7% to trade at 22.63 euros/MWh.

Further along the curve, German Cal 19' European benchmark edged 0.5% lower at 41.97 euros/MWh. The French equivalent contract closed 0.6% down at 46.81 euros/MWh.

EU carbon prices recovered from a mid-afternoon sell-off on Friday, with prices ending a bit lower in choppy trade after a strong auction. The carbon contract expiring in 2019 settled 0.3% down at 15.43 euros/tonne, posting a 4.3% weekly gain.
