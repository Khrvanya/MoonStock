Daily (31.05.2018) Brent crude jumped by 2.8% on Wednesday 
31052018

1152
DailyOil prices surged on Wednesday, as Russia's central bank expressed caution on plans to increase oil supply. Brent crude gained $2.11, or 2.8% to close at $77.50 a barrel. WTI futures added $1.48, or 2.2% to end at $68.21 a barrel.British wholesale gas prices hiked on Wednesday as lower wind output boosted demand for gas from power stations. UK day-ahead gas price rose by 1.2 pence, or 2.1% to close at 59.45 p/therm. Winter 2018 delivery contract settled 1.6% higher at 65.85 p/therm.

European wholesale power prices fell on Wednesday tracking lower coal and carbon emissions prices. The German day-ahead power price tumbled by 9.5% to trade at 42.25 euros/MWh. The French equivalent contract traded 4.2% down at 45.95 euros/MWh.

Further along the curve, German Cal 19' European benchmark edged 0.5% lower at 42.13 euros/MWh. The French equivalent contract closed 0.3% down at 46.78 euros/MWh.

EU carbon prices declined by 2.8% on Wednesday as a stronger auction had only temporary bullish effect due to wider financial market turmoil caused by Italy's political chaos. The carbon contract expiring in 2019 settled 2.8% down at 16.02 euros/tonne.
