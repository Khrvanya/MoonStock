Siemens to deliver power island for Yerevan 2 in Armenia 
01072019

0840
Electricity MarketGlobal technology company Siemens has received a contract to deliver a power island for the new Yerevan 2 combined cycle power plant (CCPP) in Armenia.Siemens' complete power island includes an SGT5-2000E gas turbine, an SST-600 steam turbine, two SGen-100A generators, heat recovery steam generator and SPPA-T3000 control system. The company will also operate and maintain the plant for a period of 20 years.
 
The CCPP will be built by Italian engineering, procurement, and construction (EPC) company Renco with a special-purpose project company, ArmPower, specifically established for this project.
 
With an electrical capacity of 250MW, Yerevan 2 combined cycle power plant is expected to commence its operations by mid-2021.
Source: power-technology.com
