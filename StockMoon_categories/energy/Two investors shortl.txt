Two investors shortlisted for Greece gas grid DESFA sale 
25092017

1141
Energy MarketGreece's privatisation agency (HRADF) selected two bidders on Friday for a 66 percent stake in the state natural gas grid operator DESFA.
The shortlisted candidates to participate in the following phase of the tender are Spain's Regasificadora del Noroeste and a conglomerate of Italy's Snam, Spain's Enagas Internacional, Belgium's Fluxys and Dutch Gasunie.

Six expressions of interest have been obtained by Greece for DESFA.

The two competitors will have to undergo the necessary procedures and will get access to DESFA's infrastructure before presenting their binding bids.

Pursuing its obligatory privatisation scheme, Greece is to complete the deal by the end of this year. Greece, hit the eighth year by the deep economic crisis, targets 2 billion euros ($2.4 billion) in asset sales in the current year and 3.5 billion in 2018.
