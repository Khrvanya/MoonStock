27 States File Lawsuits Against EPA Water Rules; First 3D-Printed Office Building PlannedThe job of 3D-printing the world's first office building is being tackled and 27 states have filed a lawsuit against the EPA over new water rules.Plans have been made to 3D-print a 2,000-square-foot office building - along with the furniture for its clients. Shanghai design company Winsun Global will work with architecture firm Gensler, structural engineer Thornton Tomasetti and construction manager Syska Hennessy Group on the project and plans to use reinforced concrete, fiber-reinforced plastic and glass-fiber-reinforced gypsum in a 20-foot-tall 3D printer.
