Output during the aforementioned period marked a 6.8% increase from the 26.3 million net tons during the same period last year (when the capability utilization rate was 76.4%).
Meanwhile, for the single week ending on April 13, raw steel production hit 1.9 million net tons -- up 7.6% from the same week in 2018 -- at a capability utilization rate of 82.3%.
