AK Steel Raises Stainless Steel Prices, LME Nickel Up TooAs nickel, a key ingredient in stainless steel, prices rise, steelmakers are increasing their prices for the versatile metal as well.AK Steel said today that the company will increase prices for all of its stainless steel products, effective with shipments on April 1, 2014. AK Steel said the increase will be achieved through a discount reduction of two percentage points. For all products priced using net prices, including automotive exhaust products, the increase will be achieved by adding $.02 per pound to the existing prices.On the LME, the nickel spot price increased 0.2 percent to $15,665 per metric ton.
