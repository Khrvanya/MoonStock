Alcoa Completes $2.85 Billion Firth Rixson AcquisitionAlcoa has announced the completion of its acquisition of Firth Rixson, a leading jet engine components manufacturer. The company had announced the Firth Rixson aqcusition in June. The total consideration for the acquisition is $2.85 billion, consisting of $2.35 billion in cash and $500 million in stock.
