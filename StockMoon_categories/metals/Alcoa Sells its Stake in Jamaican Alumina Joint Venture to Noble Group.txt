Alcoa Sells its Stake in Jamaican Alumina Joint Venture to Noble GroupAlcoa has agreed to sell its its ownership stake in the Jamalco bauxite mining and alumina refining joint venture to the Noble Group for $140 million. The Jamalco joint venture is 55% owned by Alcoa Minerals of Jamaica and 45% owned by Clarendon Alumina Production Ltd. AMJ is part of the Alcoa World Alumina and Chemicals joint venture, owned 60% by Alcoa and 40% by Alumina Limited.
