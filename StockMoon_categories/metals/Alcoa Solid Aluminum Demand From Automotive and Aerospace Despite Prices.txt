Alcoa Solid Aluminum Demand From Automotive and Aerospace Despite PricesWhile aluminum giant Alcoa has been grappling with weak prices, the company has benefited from strong performance in its value-added businesses. Indeed, the downstream business has seen strong demand from industries such as aerospace and automotive, according to an analysis by the Motley Fool.
