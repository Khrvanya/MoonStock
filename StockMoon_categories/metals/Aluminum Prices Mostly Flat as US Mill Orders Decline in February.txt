Aluminum Prices Mostly Flat as US Mill Orders Decline in FebruaryThe North American net new aluminum mill order index in February was down 7.1% from January, the Aluminum Association said Monday. New mill orders also slipped 2.9% from February 2013, and the year-to-date index is down 2%, the group said in a report.February plate orders tumbled 22% from January, but were up 15.6% from February 2013.
