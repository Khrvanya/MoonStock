Aluminum Slips, Novelis Gets First 90% Recycled Can CustomerOn the LME, the 3-month price of aluminum fell 0.8 percent to $1,815 per metric ton. The primary aluminum cash price weakened by 0.5 percent on the LME, settling at $1,785 per metric ton. Novelis got its first customer for evercan, the first 90 percent recycled aluminum beverage can on the market. Red Hare craft beer is packaging its products in cans made of Novelis' evercan aluminum sheet, which is made of a guaranteed minimum 90 percent recycled content, is expected to be on store shelves beginning in May 2014.
