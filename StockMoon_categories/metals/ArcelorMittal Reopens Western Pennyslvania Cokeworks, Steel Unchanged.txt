ArcelorMittal Reopens Western Pennyslvania Cokeworks, Steel UnchangedThe world's largest steel producer is restarting its Monessen, Pa., coke plant, five years after the facility was idled, the Pittsburgh Post-Gazette reported. Luxembourg-based ArcelorMittal is scheduled to resume production at the plant this month. The steel billet cash price saw essentially no change on the LME for the fifth day in a row, remaining around $390.00 per metric ton.
