ArcelorMittal Will Shut Down East Chicago Steel Bar Plant, 300 Jobs at StakeArcelorMittal plans to shut down a 114-year-old facility in East Chicago, Ind., which would impact more than 300 workers. The company says the "great majority" of those workers could move to other facilities.
