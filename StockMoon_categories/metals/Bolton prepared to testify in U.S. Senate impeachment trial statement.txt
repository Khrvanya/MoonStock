Bolton prepared to testify in U.S. Senate impeachment trial statementWASHINGTON (Reuters) - Former U.S. National Security Adviser John Bolton said on Monday that he is prepared to testify in a possible Senate impeachment trial of President Donald Trump if he receives a subpoena.  "I have concluded that, if the Senate issues a subpoena for my testimony, I am prepared to testify," Bolton said in a statement.  Reporting by Lisa Lambert and Susan Heavey; Editing by Chizu Nomiyama
