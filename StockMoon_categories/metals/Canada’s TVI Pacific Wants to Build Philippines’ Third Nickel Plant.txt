Inc., which in October began shipping ore to China from its newly developed Agata mine in Surigao province in the southern Philippines, expects a feasibility study on the plant to be completed this month. By building a processing plant, TVI is insulating its ability to ship ore out if the Philippines ever adopts a raw ore ban such as the one Indonesia passed in January.
