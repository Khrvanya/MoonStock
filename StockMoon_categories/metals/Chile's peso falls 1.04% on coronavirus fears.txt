Chile's peso falls 1.04% on coronavirus fearsSANTIAGO, Feb 24 (Reuters) - Chile's peso sank by 1.05% on Monday amid fears about the spread of the coronavirus. The peso fell to 810.70/811 per dollar. Italy, South Korea and Iran reported sharp rises in coronavirus cases on Monday. (Reporting by Froilan Romero and Natalia Ramos; writing by Aislinn Laing Editing by Chizu Nomiyama )
