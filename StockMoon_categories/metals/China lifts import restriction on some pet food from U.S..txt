China lifts import restriction on some pet food from U.S.BEIJING (Reuters) - China has removed a ban on imports of pet food containing ruminant ingredients from the United States, according to a notice released by customs.  The ban would take immediate effect, the General Administration of Customs said in the notice dated Feb.19.  China also approved imports of beef, beef products and live poultry from the U.S. this month. Reporting by Min Zhang and Shivani Singh; Editing by Edmund Blair
