China’s CCB Wants to Buy Metdist Trading Ltd, Gold Pulls BackA major Chinese bank sought to buy a trader with top-tier London Metal Exchange access and gold's rally paused a bit this morning.China Construction Bank (CCB) is in talks to buy UK firm Metdist Trading Limited, which would give the country's second-largest lender by assets access to top-tier trading on the London Metal Exchange, 2 metal industry sources told Reuters.
