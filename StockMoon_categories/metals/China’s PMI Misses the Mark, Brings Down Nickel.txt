China’s PMI Misses the Mark, Brings Down NickelChina's official manufacturing Purchasing Managers' Index fell to 49.8 last month from 50.1 in December, according to figures released Sunday in Beijing. That missed the median estimate of 50.2 in a Bloomberg survey. The final reading of HSBC Holdings Plc and Markit Economics' China PMI was 49.7 in January, according to data released Monday.
