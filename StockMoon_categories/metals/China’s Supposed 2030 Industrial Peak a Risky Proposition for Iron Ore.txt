China’s Supposed 2030 Industrial Peak a Risky Proposition for Iron OreMining giants have wagered $120 billion on belief that steel production in China won't peak until as late as 2030. As the price of the key steelmaking raw material continued its descent to a 5-year low today, it increasingly looks like they got it wrong.
