Chinese Planning Institute $60Ton Floor for Chinese Iron Ore in 2015A leading Chinese steel industry body predicts a modest increase in steel demand for 2015 as economic growth slows down in China, according to Caixin.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering Steel/Iron Ore markets.The China Metallurgical and Industry Planning and Research Institute believes demand for steel products will reach 720 million tons next year, up 1.4%  from this year.
