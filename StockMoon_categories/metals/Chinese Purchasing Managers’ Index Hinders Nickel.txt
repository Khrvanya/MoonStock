Chinese Purchasing Managers’ Index Hinders NickelNickel closed flat on the LME over concern that demand will fall after a report showed manufacturing grew less than expected in China, the world's largest metals consumer. China's Purchasing Managers' Index was at 50.4, the National Bureau of Statistics and China Federation of Logistics and Purchasing released today. Any number over 50 reflects an increase in purchasing activity, but it was lower than the 50.5 most analysts expected, according to a survey by the Wall Street Journal.
