Commerce Imposes Duties on GOES Imports from Russia, Japan, China, othersThe Commerce Department announced it is imposing preliminary anti-dumping duties against grain-oriented electrical steel (GOES) from China, the Czech Republic, Germany, Japan, the Republic of Korea, Poland, and the Russian Federation.   These were preliminary determinations, a final determination will be made in July and September for all of the nations accused of dumping.
