Copper MMI® Copper Falls Again, Not Saved by Chinese State PurchaseAnother week of price declines for copper, the most beleaguered of commodities metals, was accentuated by economic data from both China and the US that hindered its liquidity and ability to grow in price even more. China's official state stockpiler's purchase of 200,000 tons of the red metal didn't bolster the price much, either, in the short-term.
