Copper MMI® What Chinese Economic Data Gives Copper, It Also Taketh AwayThe week in copper mirrors the entire year. Chinese monetary easing bolstered copper's price and helped it out of the rut it's been in for most of the year, yet European economic reports weakened it as the week closed.
