Companies in the US added 208,000 workers in November after a 233,000 gain in October, ADP Research Institute said yesterday. Employers added 230,000 workers last month, according to a Bloomberg News survey of economists before tomorrow's Labor Department non-farm payrolls report. That would be the 10th consecutive month the total topped 200,000.
