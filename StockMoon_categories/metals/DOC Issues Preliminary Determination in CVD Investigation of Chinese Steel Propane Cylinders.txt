(Franklin, Tennessee). The petitioners filed their cases May 22, 2018."The merchandise covered by this investigation is steel cylinders for compressed or liquefied propane gas (steel propane cylinders) meeting the requirements of, or produced to meet the requirements of, U.S. Department of Transportation (USDOT) Specifications 4B, 4BA, or 4BW, or Transport Canada Specification 4BM, 4BAM, or 4BWM, or United Nations pressure receptacle standard ISO 4706," the DOC fact sheet for the investigation notes.The DOC identified several Chinese exporters for the upper end of the countervailable duty range (145.37%):Meanwhile, the DOC identified Shandong Huanri Group Co. Ltd. for the 42.77% duty. The 42.77% duty figure was also applied to all other Chinese exporters.The DOC is scheduled to make its final determination in the investigation by March 4, 2019.
