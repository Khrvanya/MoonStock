EU Producers Want Tariff Case on Indian Stainless Steel Wire Re-OpenedThe EU has initiated an anti-absorption investigation concerning import of stainless steel wires from India.  The European Steel Association claims that export prices have decreased and there has been insufficient movement in selling prices in the EU following the imposition of the anti-dumping duties in November.
