Ex-New York Fed official joins London Bullion Market Association boardLONDON (Reuters) - The London Bullion Market Association (LBMA), which sets standards for gold and silver bullion trading and refining, has appointed former New York Federal Reserve official Simon Potter to its board of directors.  Potter becomes the third independent member of the industry association's board, which also includes representatives of banks and refineries.  Reporting by Peter Hobson; Editing by David Goodman
