Ex-Vale chief charged with murder for role in Brazil dam disaster documentsRIO DE JANEIRO (Reuters) - State prosecutors in Brazil have charged 16 individuals with homicide and environmental crimes for their role in the Brumadinho disaster last January, when a dam owned by miner Vale SA (VALE3.SA) burst, killing hundreds, charging documents showed on Tuesday. Vale's then-chie executive, Fabio Schvartsman, was among those charged, the documents showed. Reporting by Marta Nogueira Writing by Gram Slattery; Editing by Jonathan Oatis
