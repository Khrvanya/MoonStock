Federal Reserve Actions, Miners Strike Combine to Limit Weekly Precious GainsGlobal precious metals have been strongly affected this week by the ongoing platinum miners strike in South Africa and Federal Reserve actions and statements that have been bolstering the dollar and hurting gold and silver's value as hedges against a falling dollar.
