Fiat Chrysler Considers Aluminum Body for 2017 RamFiat Chrysler says it will redesign the popular Ram standard-duty pickup in 2017 and overhaul the line of pickup trucks the following year, USA Today reported. The 2017 Ram could have an aluminum body, like the 2015 Ford F-150 coming this year, Fiat Chrysler Autogroup CEO Sergio Marchionne said.
