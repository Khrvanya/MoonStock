Former top Pompeo aide expected to testify in closed U.S. House session(Reuters) - P. Michael Mckinley, a diplomat who resigned last week as an aide to U.S. Secretary of State Michael Pompeo, is expected to appear on Wednesday for a closed session with the lawmakers conducting an impeachment inquiry into President Donald Trump, a U.S. official working on the inquiry said.
