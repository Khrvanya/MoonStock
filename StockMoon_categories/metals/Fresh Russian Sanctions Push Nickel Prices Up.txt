Fresh Russian Sanctions Push Nickel Prices UpRumors of fresh sanctions against Russian individuals by both the US and EU saw nickel prices rise on the LME on Friday and they're continuing upward today after the sanctions were officially announced. Russia's Norilsk Nickel is the world's largest producer of the metal after Indonesia began enforcing an export ban in January. Demand is still strong for the alloying metal in stainless steel.
