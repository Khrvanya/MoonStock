GOES Volume Declines For Domestic Producers Have BegunThe transformer industry appears to have moved into Plan B mode, as we previously reported. This means US-based manufacturers of wound or stacked cores have begun moving operations outside of the US to Mexico or Canada to avoid countervailing and anti-dumping duties sure to go into effect later this year (after the Department of Commerce International Trade Administration rules on the claims brought by domestic producers AK Steel and Allegheny Technologies). *MetalMiner's latest GOES coil price available in this member-only article.
