Hot-Rolled Coil Up, Schumer Stumps for Import Duties AgainImports of cheap steel subsidized by Turkey and Mexico threaten the  jobs of workers at the Nucor steel plant in Auburn, New York and should be subject to import duties, U.S. Sen. Charles Schumer said today. Schumer has been pushing for duties on Turkish and Mexican imports for months and used the appearance at the Nucor plant to reiterate his support for them.
