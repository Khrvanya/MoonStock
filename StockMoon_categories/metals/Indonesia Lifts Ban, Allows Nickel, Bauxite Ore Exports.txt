Indonesia Lifts Ban, Allows Nickel, Bauxite Ore ExportsIndonesia introduced new rules last week that will allow exports of nickel ore and bauxite and concentrates of other minerals under certain conditions in a sweeping policy shift by the key global supplier, Reuters reported.Two-Month Trial: Metal Buying OutlookA ban on unprocessed ore exports was imposed in 2014 to, the thinking went, encourage investment in mills and smelters in the islands.
