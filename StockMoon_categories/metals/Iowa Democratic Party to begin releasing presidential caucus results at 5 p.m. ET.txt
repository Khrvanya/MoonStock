Iowa Democratic Party to begin releasing presidential caucus results at 5 p.m. ETWASHINGTON (Reuters) - The Iowa Democratic Party has told presidential campaigns it will release more than half of Monday's delayed caucus results at 5 p.m. ET (2200 GMT) on Tuesday. "We have always said that we have a paper trail in this process," Iowa Democratic Party Chair Troy Price told campaigns on a briefing call. "We've always had to chase down results." Price said that the results would be released as they are finalized. Reporting By Amanda Becker and Simon Lewis; Editing by Chizu Nomiyama
