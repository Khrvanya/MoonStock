Japan's Mitsubishi Materials buys 30% stake in Chile copper mineLONDON, Feb 7 (Reuters) - Japan's Mitsubishi Materials Corp (MMC) has bought a 30% stake in Mantoverde copper mine and associated copper project for $236 million to secure more concentrates for its smelters, the company said on Friday. The mine, which has 2.1 million tonnes of copper reserves, is owned by a consortium led by British investment company Audley Capital Advisors and Orion Mine Finance Group.
