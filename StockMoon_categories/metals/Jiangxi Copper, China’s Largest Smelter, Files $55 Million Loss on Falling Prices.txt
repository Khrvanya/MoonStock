Jiangxi Copper, China’s Largest Smelter, Files $55 Million Loss on Falling PricesJiangxi Copper Co., China's largest smelter of the metal, reported a 64 percent drop in first-quarter profit because of falling prices according to Bloomberg News.Net income declined to 347 million yuan ($55.5 million), or 0.10 yuan a share, in the three months ended March 31, compared with 971 million yuan, or 0.28 yuan, a year earlier, Jiangxi Copper said today in a statement.
