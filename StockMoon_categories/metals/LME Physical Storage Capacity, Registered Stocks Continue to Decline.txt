LME Physical Storage Capacity, Registered Stocks Continue to DeclineThe London Metal Exchange is seeing volumes fall while Japan and China spar over anti-dumping duties the latter slapped on the former for electrical steel imports.The number of London Metal Exchange physical storage units continues to decline in tandem with falling registered stocks. As of July 8 there were 608 registered warehouses for the storage of base metals, down from 621 a year ago and from almost 700 in 2012 and 2013.
