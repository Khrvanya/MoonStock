Lawsuits, Warehousing Disputes Highlight Aluminum’s WeekIt was another soap opera week in the intriguing, drama-filled world of aluminum. JPMorgan Chase, the London Metal Exchange and Goldman Sachs Group argued in federal court that a lawsuit charging the three with pushing aluminum prices from 2009 to 2011 should be dismissed. Meanwhile, the dispute over load-in and load-out prices on the LME for actual aluminum buyers continues unabated as the CME Group comes closer to launching its own aluminum futures contract this May.
