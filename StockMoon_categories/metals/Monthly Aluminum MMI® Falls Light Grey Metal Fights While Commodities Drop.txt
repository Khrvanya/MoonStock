Monthly Aluminum MMI® Falls Light Grey Metal Fights While Commodities DropAluminum keeps fighting the general market as it tries to hold its gains for the year while commodities, overall, are falling sharply. Although crude is not the primary source of energy in producing  aluminum, energy accounts for 30% of its total cost and lower oil prices can have a deflationary effect.Compare with November's Aluminum market report!In spite of all these bearish developments aluminum is doing okay, finishing the year with a 13% gain.
