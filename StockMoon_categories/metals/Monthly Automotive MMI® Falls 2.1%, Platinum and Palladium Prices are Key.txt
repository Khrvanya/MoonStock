Monthly Automotive MMI® Falls 2.1%, Platinum and Palladium Prices are KeyThe monthly Automotive MMI(r) registered a value of 95 in April, a decrease of 2.1 percent from 97 in March. The most chaotic metal movers over the last month have been platinum and palladium, two metals used in automotive catalytic converters. Renewed tensions in Russia, a top producer of platinum, over Ukraine and a South African Miners' Strike have colluded to make platinum's movements on the LME difficult to predict. The price of steel continued its mostly flat course of the past few months, which could mean an easier road to profitability for automakers down the road.
