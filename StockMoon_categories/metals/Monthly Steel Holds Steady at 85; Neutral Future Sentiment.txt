Monthly Steel Holds Steady at 85; Neutral Future SentimentThe monthly Raw Steels MMI(r) registered a value of 85 in April, on par with March's value. Like any other industrial metal, during the past few years, Euro-zone problems and the cooling of the Chinese economy have impacted steel demand. Moreover, expansions of domestic facilities and competition in the US from cheaper imports have lead to oversupply of steel compared to demand.
