Nickel Rises Nearly 5% on LME Today, Up Another 4.3% FridayNickel jumped nearly 5 percent on the London Metal Exchange today, extending a winning streak that has fueled gains of more than 12 percent in May alone as investors and consumers stock up on prospects of a worsening supply shortfall later in the year. This was after a 4.3 percent jump on Friday. The supply concerns center around an export ban by Indonesia, the former top nickel ore exporter and Russia's Norilsk Nickel, the largest supplier of refined nickel in the world.
