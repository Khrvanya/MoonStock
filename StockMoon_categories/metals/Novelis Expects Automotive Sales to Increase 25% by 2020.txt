Novelis Expects Automotive Sales to Increase 25% by 2020Novelis, the world's biggest supplier of aluminum sheets to automakers, expects sales to customers including Audi AG and Ford Motor Co. to rise almost three-fold as new emissions rules restrict the use of steel. Shipments of automotive aluminum may climb to as much as 25% of total sales by 2020 from the current 9% of 3 million metric tons, Novelis vice chairman Debnarayan Bhattacharya said on Thursday in an interview with the Hindu Businessline in Mumbai.
