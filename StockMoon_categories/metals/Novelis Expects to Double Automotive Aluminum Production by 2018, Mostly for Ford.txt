Novelis Expects to Double Automotive Aluminum Production by 2018, Mostly for FordNovelis expects to increase its production in North America from about 50,000 tons of automotive aluminum in 2013 to 260,000 tons in 2014. By 2015 the production will be increased up to 365,000 tons.
