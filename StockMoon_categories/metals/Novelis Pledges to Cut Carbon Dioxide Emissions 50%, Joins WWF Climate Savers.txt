Novelis Pledges to Cut Carbon Dioxide Emissions 50%, Joins WWF Climate SaversNovelis has been accepted into the World Wildlife Fund's Climate Savers program, making the aluminum rolling and recycling firm the only metals company to be accepted into the program.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering the Aluminum market.As part of its participation in the program, Novelis has established Climate Savers commitments that align with its established sustainability agenda. By 2020, the company will reduce the carbon emissions from its operations by 10 percent, reduce absolute carbon emissions by 50 percent and increase recycled content of its products to 80 percent.
