O’Neal Invests $5 million in Birmingham, Ala., Caterpillar PartnershipO'Neal Manufacturing Services is expanding its partnership with heavy equipment manufacturer Caterpillar, a move that is expected to add up to 25 jobs in Birmingham, according to Al.com. OMS also plans to invest about $5 million in new equipment for Caterpillar's needs, the company said.
