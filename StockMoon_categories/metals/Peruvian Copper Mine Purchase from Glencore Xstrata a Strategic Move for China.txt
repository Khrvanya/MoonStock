Peruvian Copper Mine Purchase from Glencore Xstrata a Strategic Move for ChinaA Wall Street Journal analysis said China's acquisition of Glencore Xstrata's Las Bambas, Peru, copper mine is a strategic acquisition. China will need a sustainable source of copper to continue to modernize its economy and urbanize its towns and cities, despite slowing economic growth. China consumes around 40% of world copper supply, a commodity used to make computers, refrigerators, cars, plumbing equipment and power lines, to name just a few applications.
