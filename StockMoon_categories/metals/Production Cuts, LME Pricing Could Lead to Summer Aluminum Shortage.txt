Production Cuts, LME Pricing Could Lead to Summer Aluminum ShortageThe ongoing LME pricing fight and higher demand for aluminum as the Spring/Summer construction season starts could lead to a domestic shortage of the price-challenged metal later this year. Alcoa, on the heels of a quarterly loss and downgrades, announced it may cut production at other facilities this week. The largest US aluminum producer has already shut down two smelters this year.
