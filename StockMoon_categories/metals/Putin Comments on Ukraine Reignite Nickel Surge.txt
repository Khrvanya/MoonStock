Putin Comments on Ukraine Reignite Nickel SurgeRussian President Vladimir Putin's recent comments keeping force on the table as an option in the east-west power struggle over Ukraine has further bolstered speculation among traders investing in nickel. Russia's Norilsk Nickel is the world's top producer of nickel and a January export ban in Indonesia shows no signs of a lift.
