Rare Earths EPA Slaps Molycorp with Hazardous Waste FineThe US Environmental Protection Agency announced Monday that it has fined North America's sole rare earths producer, Molycorp Minerals, $27,300 for improper management of hazardous waste at its Mountain Pass, California, facility.
