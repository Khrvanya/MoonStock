Raw Steels MMI® Nucor, AK Steel Report Strong Results, Severstahl Sells AssetsWhile it was another steady to down week in raw steels, US steelmakers all reported earnings that beat Wall Street expectations this week. Nucor increased sales 4% in the quarter, while AK Steel slashed its quarterly loss to beat the Street.While prices have stayed flat for most of this year in the raw steels markets, steelmakers have shown strong results in both quarters now through economies of scale and leaner operations.
