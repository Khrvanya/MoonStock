Renewables MMI® California Tries Giving Away Silicon Solar PanelsThanks to fees from its state cap-and-trade law, Northern California homeowners will soon start receiving completely free crystalline silicon photovoltaic solar panels.Why Manufacturers Need to Ditch Purchase Price VarianceOakland nonprofit Grid Alternatives is using $14.7 million raised through the state's cap-and-trade system to install the panels in lower income neighborhoods for free. The fees were paid by industries whose emissions exceeded the state "cap" set by the new law.
