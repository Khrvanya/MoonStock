Rio Tinto’s Cote Abruptly Quits the Aluminum Mining CompanyThe world's second-largest mining company, said the head of its aluminum division Jacynthe Cote has quit for personal reasons to pursue other interests.Alfredo Barrios, 48, will replace Cote from June 1. He joins the company after a 22-year stint with BP where he was most recently a senior executive at its TNK-BP venture, London-based Rio said today in a statement.
