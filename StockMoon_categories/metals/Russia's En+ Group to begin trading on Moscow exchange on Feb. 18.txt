Russia's En+ Group to begin trading on Moscow exchange on Feb. 18MOSCOW, Feb 17 (Reuters) - Shares in Russia's En+ Group will begin trading on the Moscow exchange on Tuesday, the exchange said on Monday. Aluminium and hydropower group En+ this month said it will buy back 21.4% of its shares from state bank VTB for $1.6 billion as it seeks to simplify its ownership structure.
