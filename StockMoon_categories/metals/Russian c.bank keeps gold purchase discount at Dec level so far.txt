Russian c.bank keeps gold purchase discount at Dec level so farMOSCOW, Feb 14 (Reuters) - Russian central bank has so far this year kept a discount to its price for domestic gold purchases at December's level, it told Reuters on Friday. This discount here was imposed in May and grew gradually each month. It reached its peak in December.
