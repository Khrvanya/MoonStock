Saudi Arabia Won’t Reduce Oil Production, Reliance Steel & Aluminum Purchases TSIHope is fading for a cut in OPEC oil production. Reliance Steel & Aluminum recently announced a major acquisition.Saudi Arabia and its Gulf allies will reject calls from fellow OPEC members to cut oil output, Iran said on Wednesday, two days before the group meets amid falling prices and a worsening global oil glut.Free Sample Report: Our Annual Metal Buying Outlook"It is unlikely that these countries voluntarily cut their output," Iran's OPEC governor Mehdi Asali told Iranian news agency Shana. Iran is preparing to ramp up output after an expected lifting of Western sanctions on Tehran next year, and wants OPEC to accommodate the new volume.Los Angeles distributor Reliance Steel & Aluminum Co. will purchase all of the outstanding capital stock of Tubular Steel Inc. of St. Louis, a distributor and processor of carbon, alloy and stainless steel pipe, tubing and bar products.Free Download: The November MMI ReportThe addition of TSI, which was founded in 1953, broadens Reliance's product breadth and supports its customer diversification strategy, a statement said.
