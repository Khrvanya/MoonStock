Sherritt to Sell Coal Business to Focus on Nickel and OilSherritt International Corp is moving on. The Canadian miner "posted a bigger fourth-quarter loss due to an impairment charge of C$466.8 million ($426 million) related to the pending sale of its coal mines, and it slashed its quarterly dividend," reports Reuters."Sherritt is selling its coal business for C$946 million to focus on nickel and oil.
