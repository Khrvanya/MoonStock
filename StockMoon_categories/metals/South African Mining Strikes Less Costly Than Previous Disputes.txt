Australian imports fell 21.8% compared to July. For the first 8 months of the year, China's total coal imports were 48.64 mmt, down 20.4% compared to the same year-ago period.Strikes cost South Africa 6 billion rand, or $453 million, in wages in 2014, lower than the previous year despite a record 5-month strike in the mining sector, a report published by South Africa's Department of Labour showed on Monday.
