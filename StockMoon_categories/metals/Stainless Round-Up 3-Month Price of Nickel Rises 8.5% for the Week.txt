Stainless Round-Up 3-Month Price of Nickel Rises 8.5% for the WeekThe week's biggest mover on the weekly Stainless MMI(r) was the nickel 3-month price, which saw a 8.5 percent increase on the LME to $14,560 per metric ton. This comes on the heels of a 4.0 percent decline the week prior. The spot price of nickel rose 8.4 percent on the LME to $14,485 per metric ton after falling 3.9 percent during the previous week.
