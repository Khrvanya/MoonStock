Stainless Steel Round-Up Chinese Primary Nickel Falls 1.6%The week's biggest mover on the weekly Stainless MMI(r) was the price of Chinese primary nickel, which saw a 1.6 percent decline. This comes on the heels of a 4.1 percent increase the week before. Chinese 304 stainless steel scrap remained essentially flat from the previous week. Prices for Chinese 316 stainless steel scrap remained constant, closing the week. Chinese 304 stainless coil traded sideways last week.
