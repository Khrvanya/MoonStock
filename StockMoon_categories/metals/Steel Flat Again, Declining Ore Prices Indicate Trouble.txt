Steel Flat Again, Declining Ore Prices Indicate TroubleGlobal iron ore producers are putting up a brave front in the face of falling prices, Live Mint reported recently. Slow growth in steel production and an increase in supply have hindered steel this year. The only silver lining, if there is one, is that new supply is coming in at relatively lower costs.
