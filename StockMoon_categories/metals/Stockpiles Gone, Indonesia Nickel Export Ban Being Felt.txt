Stockpiles Gone, Indonesia Nickel Export Ban Being FeltAs a precautionary measure, Chinese nickel pig iron producers and traders stocked up on Indonesian ore and refined nickel towards the end of last year, accumulating enough material to last around nine months, according to the Financial Times. These inventories have allowed Chinese factories to keep operating this year when the Indonesian exports ceased after the domestic export ban went into effect in January.
