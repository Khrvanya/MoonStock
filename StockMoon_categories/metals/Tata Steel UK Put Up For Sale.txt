Tata Steel UK Put Up For SaleToday, we visit India where major steelmaker Tata has decided to sell its beleaguered U.K. business. The government is also extending steel safeguards to defend against cheap Chinese imports.India's Tata Steel, Britain's largest steelmaker, put its entire U.K. business up for sale to stem heavy losses, a move that would draw a line under its almost decade-long foray into Great Britain's declining steel industry.
