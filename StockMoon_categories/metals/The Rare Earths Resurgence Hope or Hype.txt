The Rare Earths Resurgence Hope or HypeRare earths got a spotlight this that's more often reserved for steel, renewables and other more flashy metals courtesy of CBS News' "60 Minutes.Response has been mostly positive and the monthly Rare Earths MMI(r) registered a value of 29 in April, an increase of 3.6% from 28 in March. More importantly, shares of the only US-based Rare Earths producer, Colo.-based Molycorp, shot up as soon as the report aired.Is this a sustainable trend?
