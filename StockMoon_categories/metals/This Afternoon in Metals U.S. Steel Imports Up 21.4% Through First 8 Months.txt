Through the first eight months of 2017, the largest offshore suppliers were South Korea (2,683,000 NT, down 1% from the same period in 2016), Turkey (1,855,000 NT, up 9%) and Japan (1,044,000 NT, down 18%).Copper has been on a tear, earlier this week hitting a three-year high.But can it last? Is the metal due for a market correction?A report on nasdaq.com speculated about the future of the metal."While copper has lately been enjoying a stellar run, analysts are skeptical about the sustainability of the recent price rally," the report states.
