"Today's filing is the culmination of a concerted effort by the AEC and its members in conjunction with Customs and the Department of Commerce to investigate Zhongwang's alleged attempts to avoid paying duties since the orders went into effect. The AEC will continue to assist the agencies in their efforts to investigate this matter going forward."Furthermore, this should be a clear signal to those that seek to evade, circumvent, or in any way violate our orders that such activities will be uncovered and prosecuted."According to the civil complaint in the Department of Justice investigation, California company Perfectus Aluminum Inc. is allegedly owned by Liu Zhongtian, founder and chairman of Zhongwang.Tokyo Steel announced its prices will be going up in October, Reuters reported.The price hike, which is taking place for the second month in a row, comes as a result of higher prices overseas and a tight domestic market, according to the report.The hedge fund industry is feeling good about the future of the metal industry.According to Reuters, hedge fund investment in the metals industry is at its highest since 2011.Free Download: The September 2017 MMI ReportA major reason for the bounceback?
