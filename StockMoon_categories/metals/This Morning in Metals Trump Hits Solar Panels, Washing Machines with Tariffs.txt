With North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) renegotiation talks in progress (with the sixth and final round kicking off today in Montreal) and Section 232 cases on steel and aluminum imports pending, it almost certainly won't be the last.In the week ending Jan. 20, domestic raw steel production was 1,693,000 net tons, down from the 1,736,000 net tons produced in the week ending Jan. 20, 2017, according to American Iron and Steel Institute (AISI) weekly data.
