U.S. Mint Sells Record Number of Silver EaglesThe U.S. Mint has sold a record number of silver coins this year as demand for the physical metal helped futures in New York recover more than 20%  since falling to a 5-year low early this month.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering the Precious markets.Purchases of American Eagle silver coins reached 43.051 million ounces in 2014, data on the mint's website shows. That tops last year's 42.7 million ounces, the previous all-time high, according to an e-mailed statement yesterday. There are still enough supplies to keep selling 2014-dated coins through the week starting Dec. 15, the mint estimates, Bloomberg News reported.
