U.S. stock funds lose $2.1 billion in latest week -LipperJan 2 (Reuters) - U.S. equity funds recorded outflows of $2.1 billion in the latest week, according to Lipper. U.S.-based taxable bond funds shed $699 million while U.S. money market funds attracted $21.3 billion in the week to Wednesday, Lipper data showed. (Reporting by Alden Bentley Editing by Chris Reese)
