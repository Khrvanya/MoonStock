U.S. stock funds shed $14.4 billion in week - LipperMarch 5 (Reuters) - U.S. equity funds saw outflows of $14.4 billion in the latest week, according to Lipper. U.S.-based taxable bond funds shed $8.9 billion, the first outflow in nine weeks and the biggest since January 2019, while money market funds drew $38.5 billion in the week to Wednesday, which was the largest inflow in 12 weeks, Lipper data showed. (Reporting by Alden Bentley; Editing by Sandra Maler)
