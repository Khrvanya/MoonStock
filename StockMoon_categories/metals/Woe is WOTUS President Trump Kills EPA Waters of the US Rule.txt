Woe is WOTUS President Trump Kills EPA Waters of the US RulePresident Donald Trump today instructed the Environmental Protection Agency and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers to review and reconsider a 2015 rule known as the Waters of the United States rule, a move that could ultimately make it easier for agricultural and development interests to drain wetlands and small streams.

Many states contended it was an overreach of existing statutes under the Clean Water Act.
