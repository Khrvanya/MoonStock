World Copper Conference in Chile canceled over coronavirus concerns - CescoSANTIAGO, March 2 (Reuters) - The CRU-Cesco World Copper Conference in Santiago has been canceled due to concerns over travel risks associated with the coronavirus outbreak, forum organizer Cesco told Reuters on Monday. The coronavirus, which was identified in China late last year, has quickly spread to dozens of countries, killing over 3,000 people and disrupting global business supply chains. (Reporting by Fabian Cambero and Natalia Ramos, writing by Dave Sherwood)
