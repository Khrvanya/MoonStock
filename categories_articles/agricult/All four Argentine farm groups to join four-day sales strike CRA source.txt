All four Argentine farm groups to join four-day sales strike CRA source1 Min ReadBUENOS AIRES (Reuters) - All four major Argentine farmers' groups will join a four-day crop sales strike slated for next week in protest of a recent increase in soy export taxes, a source with one of the groups, the Rural Confederations of Argentina (CRA), said on Thursday.
