Almost 16,000 suckler farmers enlisted to BEEP so farAs at close of business on Thursday, February 21, the second last day for applications for the Beef Environmental Efficiency Pilot (BEEP) scheme, applications received totalled 15,807 entrants.
Today, Friday, February 22, is the final day on which applications can be made to the BEEP scheme, which was announced at the end of last month.
Farmers are able to apply online via www.agfood.ie; the Department of Agriculture, Food and the Marine says that applications will not be accepted after today.
Farmers will then have between March 8 and November 1 to submit the weight data of unweaned calves and their dams, for which the department is offering EUR40 per cow-calf pairing.
The payments will be made throughout the month of December this year.
