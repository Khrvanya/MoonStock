Auction report Betting on ‘blue’ at big February gatheringMonday, February 11, saw the latest monthly Cambridge Machinery Sales auction take place at Cheffins Auctioneers' sale grounds in Sutton, Cambridge (England).
This report focuses on the older Ford / New Holland tractors present - ranging (in this report) from an original-looking 4600 up to a couple of (white-roof; SLE) 8240 models.
In accompanying articles, we look at a variety of other big-name brands.
Tim Scrivener was at the auction for AgriLand to take these photographs and to note the key prices. All of the pictures in this report are from Monday's auction.
