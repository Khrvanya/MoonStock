Auction report Brace of ‘blues’ – big and small – under the hammerMonday, February 11, saw the latest monthly Cambridge Machinery Sales auction take place at Cheffins Auctioneers' sale grounds in Sutton, Cambridge (England).
Here, we focus on some of the (more modern) New Holland / Ford tractors present - ranging (in this report) from small units with no cabs right the way up the power spectrum.

In accompanying articles, we look at a variety of other big-name brands.
Tim Scrivener was at the auction for AgriLand to take these photographs and to note the key prices. All of the pictures in this report are from Monday's auction.
