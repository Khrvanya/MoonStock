Auction report Check out this mixed bag of John DeeresMonday, February 11, saw the latest monthly Cambridge Machinery Sales auction take place at Cheffins Auctioneers' sale grounds in Sutton, Cambridge (England).
This report focuses on some of the John Deere tractors present.
In accompanying articles, we look at a variety of other big-name brands.
Tim Scrivener was at the auction for AgriLand to take these photographs and to note the key prices. All of the pictures in this report are from Monday's auction.
