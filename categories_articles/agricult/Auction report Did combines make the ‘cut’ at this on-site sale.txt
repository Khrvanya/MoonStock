Auction report Did combines make the ‘cut’ at this on-site saleAn on-site auction of tractors and farm machinery took place at Washpit Farm, Grimston, King's Lynn (England), on Wednesday, February 20.
The sale involved some noteworthy lots, including two combine harvesters and several high-horsepower tractors, as well as a myriad of other items.
The auction was conducted by Cruso & Wilkin.

This report focuses on the combine harvesters and self-propelled equipment (including some of the older tractors). Look out for accompanying reports, in which we detail what other lots (including the newer tractors) sold for.
