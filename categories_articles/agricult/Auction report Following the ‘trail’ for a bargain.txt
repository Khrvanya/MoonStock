Auction report Following the ‘trail’ for a bargainAn on-site auction of tractors and farm machinery took place at Washpit Farm, Grimston, King's Lynn (England), on Wednesday, February 20.
The sale involved some noteworthy lots, including two combines harvesters and several high-horsepower tractors, as well as a myriad of other items.
The auction was conducted by Cruso & Wilkin.

This report focuses on the trailers that were up for grabs. Look out for accompanying reports, in which we detail what other lots sold for.
