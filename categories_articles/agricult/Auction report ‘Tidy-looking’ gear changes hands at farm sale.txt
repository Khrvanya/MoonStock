Auction report ‘Tidy-looking’ gear changes hands at farm saleAn on-site auction of tractors and farm machinery took place at Hose Lodge Farm, Nottinghamshire (England), on Thursday, February 21.
The sale involved some noteworthy lots. It was conducted by Brown & Co.
