Belaruskali to sign new potash supply deal with China in 20201 Min ReadMINSK, Jan 14 (Reuters) - Belarusian state-owned potash producer Belaruskali plans to sign a new potash supply contract with China this year, chief executive Ivan Golovaty said on Tuesday. Belaruskali's potash operations are currently operating at 75-80% capacity, the executive added. Belarus is one of the world's largest potash exporters.
