Brazil meatpacker Marfrig signs agreement to invest 600 mln reais in plant expansion - statement1 Min ReadSAO PAULO, Dec 6 (Reuters) - Brazilian meatpacker Marfrig SA said it has reached an agreement with the government of Sao Paulo to invest 600 million reais ($142.98 million) in a potential plant expansion, according to a statement on Friday. Following the expansion, the plant will have a processing capacity of 100,000 tonnes of animal and vegetal proteins, Marfrig said. $1 = 4.1964 reais
