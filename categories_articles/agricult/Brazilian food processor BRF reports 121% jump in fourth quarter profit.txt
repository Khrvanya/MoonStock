Brazilian food processor BRF reports 121% jump in fourth quarter profit1 Min ReadSAO PAULO, March 3 (Reuters) - Brazilian food processor BRF SA reported a 121% surge in fourth-quarter net income on Tuesday, to 690 million reais ($154 million), on strong international and domestic sales. BRF also reported adjusted EBITDA - earnings before interest, tax, depreciation and amortization, a measure of operating income - of 1.413 billion reais in the latest quarter, an almost 68% rise from a year earlier. $1 = 4.4738 reais
