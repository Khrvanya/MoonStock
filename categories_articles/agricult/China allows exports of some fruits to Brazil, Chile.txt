China allows exports of some fruits to Brazil, Chile1 Min ReadBEIJING, Jan 6 (Reuters) - China's customs said on Monday that it has allowed exports of pears to Brazil. China has also allowed exports of kiwifruits to the Republic of Chile, its customs said in a separate statement on the same day.
