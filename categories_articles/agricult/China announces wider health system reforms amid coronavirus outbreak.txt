China announces wider health system reforms amid coronavirus outbreak1 Min ReadBEIJING, March 5 (Reuters) - China will take steps to improve and promote commercial health insurance, centralise drug procurement and promote generic drugs, as part of deepening health sector reforms unveiled amid the coronavirus crisis, the official Xinhua news agency reported on Thursday.
