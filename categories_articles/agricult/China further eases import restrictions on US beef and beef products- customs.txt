China further eases import restrictions on US beef and beef products- customs1 Min ReadBEIJING, Feb 24 (Reuters) - China has lifted a ban on beef and beef products from American cows more than 30 months old, according to a notice released by the customs office on Monday. China first lifted a 14-year ban on U.S. beef in 2017, allowing imports of deboned and boned beef from American cows under 30 months old. Reporting by Hallie Gu and Shivani Singh; Editing by Alex
