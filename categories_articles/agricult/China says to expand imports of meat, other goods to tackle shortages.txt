China says to expand imports of meat, other goods to tackle shortages1 Min ReadBEIJING, Feb 10 (Reuters) - China will expand imports of meat and other goods key for the public's livelihood in order to address shortages, an official at its commerce ministry said on Monday, amid a coronavirus outbreak that has disrupted the country's economy. Zhu Xiaoliang, director-general of the ministry's market system development, made the comments during an online briefing.
