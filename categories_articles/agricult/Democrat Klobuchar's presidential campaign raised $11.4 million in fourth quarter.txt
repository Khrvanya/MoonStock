Democrat Klobuchar's presidential campaign raised $11.4 million in fourth quarter1 Min ReadWASHINGTON (Reuters) - U.S. Senator Amy Klobuchar, who is running for the Democratic nomination for president, raised $11.4 million in the fourth quarter of 2019, more than doubling the previous quarter's amount, according to a statement from her campaign.  Klobuchar's polling numbers have increased in recent weeks after two highly-praised debate performances.
