Fonterra's Nov milk output in Australia falls1 Min ReadJan 31 (Reuters) - Dairy giant Fonterra said on Friday milk production in Australia dropped 3.4% in November, blaming challenging farm and weather conditions. Australia has been grappling with severe drought and bushfires that have hit livestock and scorched huge swathes of land.
