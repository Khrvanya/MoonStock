Ghana G&S cocoa arrivals reach 208,000 T by Nov. 14 - COCOBOD1 Min ReadABIDJAN, Dec 2 (Reuters) - Ghana's graded and sealed (G&S) cocoa arrivals reached 208,000 tonnes between the start of this year's harvest on Oct. 1 and Nov. 14, down 1% from 210,000 tonnes the previous season, figures from marketing board Cocobod showed on Monday. G&S is cocoa that has been quality checked and sealed in bags by Cocobod and is ready to be shipped. Cocobod expects production to reach 850,000 tonnes this season.
