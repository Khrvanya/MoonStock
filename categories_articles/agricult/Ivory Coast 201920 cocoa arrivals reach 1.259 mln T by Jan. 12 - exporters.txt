Ivory Coast 201920 cocoa arrivals reach 1.259 mln T by Jan. 12 - exporters1 Min ReadABIDJAN, Jan 13 (Reuters) - Cocoa arrivals at ports in top grower Ivory Coast reached 1.259 million tonnes between Oct. 1 and Jan. 12, 2020, exporters estimated on Monday, up 1.9% from 1.235 million tonnes in the same period last season. About 29,000 tonnes of beans were delivered to Abidjan port and 34,000 tonnes to San Pedro between Jan. 6 and Jan. 12 for a total of 63,000 tonnes, up from 60,000 tonnes during the same week last season.
