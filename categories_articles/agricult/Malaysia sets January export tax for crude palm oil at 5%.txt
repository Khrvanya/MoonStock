Malaysia sets January export tax for crude palm oil at 5%1 Min ReadSINGAPORE, Dec 13 (Reuters) - Malaysia has set its export tax for crude palm oil at 5% for January, the Malaysian Palm Oil Board said on Friday. The world's second-largest producer and exporter of palm oil calculated a reference price of 2,571.16 ringgit ($616.59) per tonne for next month.
