Moy Park appoints new head of talent and employee experienceNorthern Ireland's largest agri-food company Moy Park has announced the appointment of Nicky Taylor as Head of Talent and Employee Experience.
Ms. Taylor joins poultry processor Moy Park to support its strategic growth plans and will be responsible for spearheading leadership and talent development across the business.
With over 17 years of experience, she has a strong track record of leadership and talent development in the food industry.
Also Read: Northern Ireland's Top 100 sees a strong year for agri-food...but who made the cut?
Ms. Taylor previously held the role of head of organisational development at Samworth Brothers and most recently head of leadership and talent development at Tulip.
She is also an active member of the Council for City & Guilds, the National Industry Skills Board and the Harper Adams Meat Advisory Board.

Moy Park, human resources and performance director Kirsty Wilkins said: "Moy Park place is a great deal of importance on talent, which is critical to the future growth and success of the business.
With an innovative and passionate approach to talent development, Nicky brings a wealth of experience and her appointment represents a significant investment in our people.
Speaking about her appointment, Ms. Taylor said: "Moy Park prides itself on recruiting and retaining the brightest and best talent, creating a working environment in which they can thrive.
"I'm delighted to be joining an excellent team and contributing to the company's ongoing success."
