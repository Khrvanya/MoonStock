  News Snippets>>  Delhi will host 48C, India's first public art festival between December 12 and 21. The theme of the exhibition is ecology and it will have a special focus on Delhi. The festival has been organized by the Delhi Government, Max Mueller Bhawan, GTZ, a Germany-based international group which works on sustainable development, and the Urban Resource Group, a team of architects from Delhi.
