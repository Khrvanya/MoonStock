Pelosi to hold news conference on USMCA -statement1 Min ReadWASHINGTON, Dec 10 (Reuters) - U.S. House Speaker Nancy Pelosi will hold a news conference on Tuesday to discuss the trade agreement between the United States, Mexico and Canada, her office said in a statement. Pelosi will be accompanied by Representative Richard Neal, the chairman of the Ways and Means Committee in the Democratic-led House of Representatives.
