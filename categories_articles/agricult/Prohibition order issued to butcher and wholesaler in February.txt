Prohibition order issued to butcher and wholesaler in FebruaryA butcher shop and a food wholesale and distributing business were issued with prohibition orders by the Food Safety Authority of Ireland (FSAI) in February.
The two companies were earmarked among eight enforcement orders served by the authority to food businesses last month.
The eight orders included four closure orders, two improvement orders and two prohibition orders in total.
Two prohibition orders were served under the EC (Official Control of Foodstuffs) Regulations, 2010 on:

The Friendly Meat Shop, a butcher shop based at Oldtown Road, Letterkenny, Co. Donegal; and
Miss Fit Enterprises Limited, a wholesaler and distributor at Unit 14, Block P Greenogue Business Park, Rathcoole, Co. Dublin.The two companies were earmarked among eight enforcement orders served by the authority to food businesses last month.
