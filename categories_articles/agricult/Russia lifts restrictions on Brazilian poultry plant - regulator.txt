Russia lifts restrictions on Brazilian poultry plant - regulator1 Min ReadMOSCOW, Dec 13 (Reuters) - Russia's agriculture safety watchdog said on Friday it would lift temporary restrictions on poultry supplies to Russia from a plant in Brazil on Dec. 16. The plant is operated by Brazil's Lar Cooperativa Agroindustrial, the Russian regulator, Rosselkhoznadzor, said in a statement.
