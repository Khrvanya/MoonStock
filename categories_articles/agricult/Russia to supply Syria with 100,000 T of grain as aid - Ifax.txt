Russia to supply Syria with 100,000 T of grain as aid - Ifax1 Min ReadMOSCOW, Dec 17 (Reuters) - Russia will provide Syria with 100,000 tonnes of grain as humanitarian aid, Russian news agency Interfax cited Deputy Prime Minister Yury Borisov as saying on Tuesday. Borisov said the supply would begin by the end of December, according to Interfax.
