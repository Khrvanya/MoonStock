Russia's VTB to seek investors for grains business in February - Interfax1 Min ReadMOSCOW, Jan 23 (Reuters) - Russia's VTB plans to attract one or more investors to its grain business in February, news agency Interfax reported on Thursday, citing its first deputy chief executive. Those investors are likely to be Russian partners, Interfax quoted VTB's Yuri Soloviev as saying. (Reporting by Polina Devitt; Editing by Jan Harvey)All quotes delayed a minimum of 15 minutes.
