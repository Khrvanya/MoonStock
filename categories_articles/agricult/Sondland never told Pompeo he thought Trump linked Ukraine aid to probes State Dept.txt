Sondland never told Pompeo he thought Trump linked Ukraine aid to probes State Dept1 Min ReadWASHINGTON (Reuters) - U.S. Ambassador to the European Union Gordon Sondland, who testified on Wednesday in the House impeachment inquiry, never told Secretary of State Mike Pompeo he believed President Donald Trump was linking Ukraine aid to investigations of political opponents, a State Department spokeswoman said. "Any suggestion to the contrary is flat out false," spokeswoman Morgan Ortagus said in a statement.  Reporting by Eric Beech; Editing by Tim AhmannAll quotes delayed a minimum of 15 minutes.
