U.S. House to consider USMCA trade pact on Thursday -Democratic leader1 Min ReadWASHINGTON, Dec 17 (Reuters) - The U.S. House of Representatives will consider legislation to implement the USMCA trade pact with Canada and Mexico on Thursday, the No. 2 Democrat in the chamber said. Representative Steny Hoyer, the House majority leader, made the comment during a briefing for reporters on Tuesday.
