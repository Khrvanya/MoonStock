U.S. forecaster sees 70% chance for neutral weather during winter1 Min Read(Reuters) - There is an about 70% chance of El Nino-Southern Oscillation (ENSO) neutral conditions prevailing during the 2019-20 Northern Hemisphere winter, a U.S. government weather forecaster said on Thursday. The chances of the neutral weather pattern continuing through spring 2020 is 65%, the National Weather Service's Climate Prediction Center (CPC) said in its monthly forecast.
