Ukraine reports first H5 bird flu case in three years - OIE1 Min ReadPARIS, Jan 20 (Reuters) - Ukraine has reported an outbreak of the highly pathogenic H5 bird flu virus on a farm in the west-central part of the country, the first of such outbreak in nearly three years, the World Organisation for Animal Health (OIE) said on Monday. Citing a report from Ukraine's veterinary services, the OIE said the virus was of serotype H5 but gave no further detail.
