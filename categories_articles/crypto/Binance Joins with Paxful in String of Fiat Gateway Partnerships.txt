Binance Joins with Paxful in String of Fiat Gateway PartnershipsMalta-based cryptocurrency exchange Binance, which is the world's largest cryptocurrency trading platform by number of users and trading volume, is announcing a strategic partnership with Estonian peer-to-peer Bitcoin marketplace Paxful.As of today, Paxful's Virtual Bitcoin Kiosk will be integrated into Binance, allowing Binance users to utilize Paxful's p2p network to buy Bitcoin on Binance with 167 various fiat currencies.Discover iFX EXPO Asia 2020 in Macao - The Largest Financial B2B ExpoThese include the Russian Ruble (RUB), Vietnamese Dong (VND), Indonesian Rupiah (IDR), Nigerian Naira (NGN), Colombian Peso (COP), British Pound (GBP), Mexican Peso (MXN), Canadian Dollar (CAD), Euro (EUR), and the Argentine Peso (ARS).The partnership between the two platforms follows a series of clear efforts toward the expansion of fiat-to-crypto gateway options from Binance.The exchange recently announced its acquisition of India-based cryptocurrency exchange WazirX, opening up an Indian Rupee (INR) fiat gateway.In November, Binance also partnered with payments service Papara to offer deposits and withdrawals of the Turkish lira (TRY), and with Advcash to offer deposits and withdrawals of Euros (EUR), Russian Rubles (RUB), Ukrainian hryvnia (UAH) and Kazakhstani Tenge (KZT.)
