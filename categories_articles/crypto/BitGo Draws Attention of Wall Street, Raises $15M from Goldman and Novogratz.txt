BitGo Draws Attention of Wall Street, Raises $15M from Goldman and NovogratzCryptocurrency wallet provider BitGo has raised $15 million in new funding, which will be put towards expanding the use of its technology among institutional investors, the company announced Thursday.Including the new funding, BitGo has raised $58.5 million for its Series B round, making it one of the most well-funded companies in the cryptocurrency industry.
