FCA Clarifies Crypto Guidelines with New Consultation PaperThe Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) on Wednesday released a consultation paper on cryptocurrencies to clarify its regulatory scope on the industry.Dubbed "Guidance on Crypto" the consultation paper is an update to similar guidelines published in January this year.
