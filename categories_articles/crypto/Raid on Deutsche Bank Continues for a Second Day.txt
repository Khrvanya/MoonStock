Raid on Deutsche Bank Continues for a Second DayThe police raid on Deutsche Bank that began yesterday is continuing for a second day, according to a report from Reuters. The large amount of material is allegedly the reason for the second day of the search.The bank said in a statement that it is "cooperating fully" with investigations.
