Per the company, it is currently managing around $70 million worth digital asset-backed investment products."For the first time, institutions, as well as retail investors, can invest in the public blockchain, the Tezos network with a PoS concept and consensus mechanism via our ETP and participate in the associated rewards for contributing to the security and stability of the Tezos blockchain - without the need to ever have to set up a crypto wallet or taking care of a private key," Rashwan added.
