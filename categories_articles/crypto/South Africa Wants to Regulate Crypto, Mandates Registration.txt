In addition, the cryptocurrency service providers would also have to monitor and report suspicious transactions, such as cash transactions of R25,000 and more.Despite the lack of proper infrastructure, African crypto adoption is booming. Last year, African Bitcoin exchange Paxful reported $40 million in monthly trading volumes.Earlier this month, South Africa appointed a working group to access cryptocurrency and blockchain concepts.
