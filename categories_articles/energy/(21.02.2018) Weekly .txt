Energy MarketHere's a quick snapshot of the main energy indicators in the last 7 days. 

 
BRENT M+1: 67.62 $/bbl
The oil price rose, driven by the comments of the Saudi Energy Minister and the decline of the U.S. dollar.
 
Outlook (horizon 1 week): A rise towards the resistance level of 66.31 $/bbl and its possible test.
 
GAS TTF Y+1: 16.56 EUR/MWh
The price rose, stimulated by the bullish rally of the oil price and the rise of the spot contracts caused by the cold weather.
 
Outlook (horizon 1 week): A rise towards the resistance level of 17.27 EUR/MWh.
 
POWER GE Y+1: 33.89 EUR/MWh 
The price rose, following the bullish movement of the coal prices and supported by the upward dynamics of the spot price.
 
Outlook (horizon 1 week): A rise towards the resistance level of 36.04 EUR/MWh.
