(23.11.2017) Weekly Energy Market Review at a Glance 
24112017

1410
Energy MarketHere's a quick snapshot of the main energy indicators in the last 7 days. 

BRENT M+1: 62.22 $/bbl
The oil price declined, driven by a bearish IEA monthly report and the rumors regarding possible tensions between Russia and the OPEC countries.
 
Outlook: A test of the resistance level of 62.63 $/bbl, followed by a further rise.
 
GAS TTF Y+1: 18.19 EUR/MWh
The price fell after the reaching of a strong resistance level and stimulated by the bearish correction of the spot price.
 
Outlook: A test of the resistance level of 18.36 EUR/MWh.
 
POWER GE Y+1: 36.02 EUR/MWh 
The price declined, influenced by the bearish correction of the coal market and by the failure of the coalition negotiations between the CDU-CSU alliance, the Greens and the Free Democratic Party.
 
Outlook: A bullish movement towards the resistance level of 36.86 EUR/MWh.
