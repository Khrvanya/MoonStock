CEZ's Dukovany unit 2 outage to take 2-3 weeks longer due to repairs 
02092019

1030
NuclearElectricity producer CEZ said an outage of its 500-megawatt Unit 2 at the Dukovany nuclear plant, where a pipe leak had been discovered, would take about two to three weeks longer that previously expected.CEZ had last said the repair would be completed by Sept. 4.
 
The unit has been offline since May for a fuel exchange.
 
A small leak was later discovered in the unit's steam generator.
Source: reuters.com
