Daily (10.04.2018) NBP spot gas price hiked by more than 2% due to declining wind generation and higher demand 
10042018

1300
DailyOil prices rose on Monday, supported by a recovery in the stock market as worries over a trade war between the United States and China eased. Brent crude added $1.54, or 2.3%, to close at $68.65 a barrel. WTI futures surged by 2.2% to settle at $63.42 a barrel.British wholesale gas prices soared on Monday due to declining wind generation which bolstered demand and as a halt to flows from a liquefied natural gas terminal left the system undersupplied. UK day-ahead gas price increased by over 2% to end at 49.65 p/therm. Meanwhile, Winter 2018 delivery contract settled 1.4% higher at 53.75 p/therm.

European prompt power prices jumped on Monday tracking coal and carbon prices. Contracts on the curve hit the highest level last seen in early January.

German baseload power price for Monday delivery hiked by 118.6% to close at 36.03 euros/MWh. The equivalent French contract ended 58.8% higher 41.79 euros/MWh.

Further along the curve, German Cal 19' European benchmark contract was 2% up at 37.35 euros/MWh, while the French equivalent contract gained 1.3% to close at 41.75 euros/MWh.

European carbon prices jumped on Monday as observers have foreseen a renewal of gains before the annual compliance deadline in the wake of the first weekly price fall of the year. The carbon contract expiring in 2019 traded 2.6% higher at 13.42 euros/tonne.
