Daily (30.04.2018) UK gas prices rose on Friday on forecasts for higher consumption and lower temperatures 
30042018

1102
DailyOil prices edged lower on Friday on supply fears as the United States could reintroduce sanctions against Iran. Brent futures fell 10 cents, or 0.1 percent, to end at $74.64 a barrel. WTI crude was 0.1% down at $68.10 a barrel.British wholesale gas prices soared on Friday on forecasts for higher gas consumption and lower temperatures. NBP spot price rose 0.85 pence, or 1.6%, to close at 53.35 p/therm. Meanwhile, 2019 delivery contract traded 1.7% higher at 50.11 p/therm.

European spot electricity prices slumped in the wholesale market on Friday, curbed by a forecast rise in wind output and a slid in consumption. German day-ahead power price declined by 27% to end at 29.09 euros/MWh. The French equivalent contract settled 40% down at 21.97 euros/MWh.

Further along the curve, German Cal 19' European benchmark added 0.2% to close at 38.81 euros/MWh. The French equivalent contract ended 0.4% higher at 43.52 euros/MWh.

European carbon prices surged on Friday despite another weak auction result. The carbon contract expiring in 2019 was 0.5% higher at 13.72 euros/tonne.
