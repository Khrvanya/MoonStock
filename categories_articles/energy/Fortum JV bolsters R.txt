Fortum JV bolsters Russian regional ties 
12102018

0919
WindFinnish energy company Fortum and local partner Rusnano have signed a cooperation agreement with the government of Kalmykia to build up to 450MW of wind power in the Russian region.The non-binding agreement covers the 2018 to 2021 period. Fortum said that a separate investment decision will be made for each project, without elaborating further.
 
The company's 50:50 joint venture with Rusnano has won the right to develop almost 2GW of wind  power in Russia.

Capacity is scheduled to come online between 2019 and 2023.
Source: renews.biz
