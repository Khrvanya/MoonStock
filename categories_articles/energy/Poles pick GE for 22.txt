Poles pick GE for 220MW Potegowo party 
12072019

0856
WindGE Renewable Energy is to provide turbines for the 220MW Potegowo wind farm in northern Poland.Potegowo will comprise four clusters, including the five-turbine Biecino, where hardware is currently being installed.
 
The deal, which included a five year service agreement, is the first between GE and developer Potegowo Mashav.
Source: renews.biz
