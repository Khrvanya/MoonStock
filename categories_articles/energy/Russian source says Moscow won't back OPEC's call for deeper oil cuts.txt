VIENNA, March 6 (Reuters) - A Russian high-level source told Reuters on Friday that Moscow would not back an OPEC call for extra reductions in oil output and would only agree to extending existing OPEC+ cuts."That position won't change," the source said as ministers from OPEC, Russia and other producer gathered for talks at OPEC's Vienna headquarters.(Reporting by Dmitry Zhdannikov; Editing by Edmund Blair)
