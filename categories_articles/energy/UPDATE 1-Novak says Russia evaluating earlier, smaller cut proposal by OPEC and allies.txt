The committee meets again on March 3.As oil prices fell below $50 per barrel last week, their lowest since July 2017, Saudi Arabia and some other OPEC members proposed to deepen the cuts by another 1 million bpd to address demand fears hit by the coronavirus outbreak, sources said.(Reporting by Olesya Astakhova, Writing by Alexander Marrow Editing by Kirsten Donovan and David Evans)
