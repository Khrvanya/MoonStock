(Releads with confirmation from the energy ministry)MOSCOW, Sept 10 (Reuters) - Russian Energy Minister Alexander Novak plans to meet Saudi Arabia's new energy minister Prince Abdulaziz bin Salman on Tuesday in Jeddah, a spokesman for the energy ministry said on Tuesday.He declined additional comments. Prince Abdulaziz bin Salman took over as energy minister from Khalid al-Falih on Sunday. Russia and Saudi Arabia are the two leading oil producers in OPEC and non-OPEC global oil production cut deal.
