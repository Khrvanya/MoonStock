Alcoa Asks for $275 Million Federal Loan for Ford F-150 Aluminum ProductionAlcoa has applied for a $275 million federal loan to expand production of automotive aluminum to meet demand for Ford's 2015 aluminum-bodied F-150 truck.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering the Aluminum market.After applying earlier this year to the US Department of Energy for a share of the remaining $16 billion in the U.S. Advanced Technology Vehicles Manufacturing fund, Alcoa completed an environmental review required to expand its Tennessee plant. The expansion would create 500 construction jobs and 200 full-time jobs, as well as enabling Alcoa to produce 640 million pounds per year of aluminum for the trucks.
