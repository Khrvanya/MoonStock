Alcoa Investing $25 Million in Virginia Jet Engine PlantAlcoa recently announced that it is expanding the Alcoa Power and Propulsion facility, located in Hampton, Va. The company will invest $25 million to scale-up a process technology that will reduce the weight of its jet engine blades by 20%, and significantly improve their aerodynamic performance.
