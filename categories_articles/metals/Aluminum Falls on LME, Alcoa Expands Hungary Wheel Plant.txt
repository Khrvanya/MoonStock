Aluminum Falls on LME, Alcoa Expands Hungary Wheel PlantThe cash price of primary Indian aluminum moved up 2.3 percent on Thursday, March 20, making it the day's biggest mover. On the LME, the primary aluminum cash price fell 1.1 percent to $1,672 per metric ton. On the LME, the 3-month price of aluminum declined 1 percent to $1,715 per metric ton.Alcoa is investing $13 million to expand its wheel manufacturing plant in Hungary, to meet growing demand for its lightweight, durable, aluminum truck wheels.The expansion of the Szekesfehervar, Hungary facility will enable Alcoa to produce twice as many surface-treated wheels in Europe by early 2015, compared with current production levels.
