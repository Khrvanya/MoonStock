Aluminum MMI® 90% Recycled Sheet Debuts, LME Tries Again on Warehouse IssueAnother week in "As the Aluminum Turns" finished with the London Metal Exchange taking another swing at fixing its load-in, load-out pricing issue by offering a regional aluminum premium contract to allow consumers such as beverage can users and automakers to hedge against the premiums paid to producers on top of the price of metal. New products such as 90% recyclable beer can sheet and 47% lighter truck wheels debuted and trusty Stuart Burns explained what it all meant to the market.
