Aluminum Up Slightly; UC Rusal in ‘Good Shape’ Despite Worst Quarter Since ’08An executive from Russian aluminum group UC Rusal said on Thursday it was in "very good shape" and will be able to cope with its debt repayment schedule. The metal producer, which reported its worst annual results since 2008 with a loss of $3.2 billlion for 2013, said that while it needed more time to come to final agreements with banks, it was confident of the outcome.
