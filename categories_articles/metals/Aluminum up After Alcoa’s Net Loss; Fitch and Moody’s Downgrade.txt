Aluminum up After Alcoa’s Net Loss; Fitch and Moody’s DowngradeAlcoa will likely recover from a recent restructuring, but more concerning is that both Fitch Ratings and Moody's Investors Service cut its rating to junk status in the wake of the report. Fitch told Bloomberg News that this year and next it expects Alcoa's profit to continue to reflect weakness in the price of aluminum, as its debt-to-Ebitda ratio rises to as much as 2.9.
