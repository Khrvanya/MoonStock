Andy Home China’s State Resources Bureau is Back And Buying CopperReuters metal columnist Andy Home ponders what, exactly, China's State Resources Bureau is up to with its purchase of 200,000 tons of copper. Home says the best estimate is that the SRB will be a net buyer of around 300,000 tons this year, albeit with the potential to add more against its 2015 budget target if the price is right.
