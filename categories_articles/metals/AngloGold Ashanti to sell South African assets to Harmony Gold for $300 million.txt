AngloGold Ashanti to sell South African assets to Harmony Gold for $300 millionJOHANNESBURG, Feb 12 (Reuters) - AngloGold Ashanti will sell its remaining South African assets for $300 million to Harmony Gold, the bullion miner said on Wednesday. AngloGold said in May that it will review divestment options from its South African assets, including the worlds deepest mine, in May as it looks to streamline its portfolio and focus on assets that deliver higher returns. (Reporting by Tanisha Heiberg; Editing by Himani Sarkar)
