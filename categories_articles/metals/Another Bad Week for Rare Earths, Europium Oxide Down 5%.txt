Another Bad Week for Rare Earths, Europium Oxide Down 5%Prices for rare earth elements continued to trade, at best, flat this week while others, such as europium oxide, saw big weekly slides. This belies the fact that while many analysts are now giving rosy forecasts about rare earths prices, that demand has generally waned while producers of cell phones, TVs and other former rare earths consumers have found other metal combinations to replace the once-sky-high-priced rare elements.
