ArcelorMittal Invests Heavily in German Automotive Steel PlantSteel giant ArcelorMittal has has switched to a new symmetrical format for continuous casting line two at its steel plant in Duisburg, Germany, offering customers in the automotive supply sector high-quality steel ingots for producing forgings. The new wire rod mill utilizes the latest technologies and processes high-strength and ultra-high-strength steels which enables thermo-mechanical rolling processes.
