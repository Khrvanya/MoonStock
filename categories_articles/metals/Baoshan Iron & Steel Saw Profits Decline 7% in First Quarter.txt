Baoshan Iron & Steel Saw Profits Decline 7% in First QuarterBaoshan Iron & Steel Co., China's biggest publicly-traded steelmaker, said profit fell 7 percent in the first quarter after steel prices dropped. Net income declined to 1.51 billion CNY ($241 million), from 1.63 billion CNY a year earlier, the company said yesterday in a statement to the Shanghai Stock Exchange.
