Baosteel, Other Chinese Steel Majors Will Reap Lower Iron Ore Price RewardsSchadenfreude is a feeling China's steelmakers should learn to enjoy. Despite their own misfortunes, their suppliers are even worse off, the Wall Street Journal reports.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering Steel/Iron Ore markets.Otherwise squeezed by too much capacity and too little demand, Chinese steel mills can celebrate the 48% collapse this year in the price of their key ingredient, iron ore.
