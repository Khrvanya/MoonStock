Boeing 777X jetliner successfully completes maiden flightSEATTLE (Reuters) - Boeing Co (BA.N) on Saturday successfully completed the maiden flight of the world's largest twin-engined jetliner, the 777X. The 252-foot-long passenger jet landed at Boeing Field near downtown Seattle at 2:00 p.m. local time (2200 GMT) after a debut of more than three hours marking the start of a year of testing before it enters intercontinental service in 2021.
