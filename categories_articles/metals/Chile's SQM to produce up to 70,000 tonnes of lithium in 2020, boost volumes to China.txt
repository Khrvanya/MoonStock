Chile's SQM to produce up to 70,000 tonnes of lithium in 2020, boost volumes to ChinaSANTIAGO, March 3 (Reuters) - Chilean miner SQM said it plans to produce as much as 70,000 tonnes of battery metal lithium in 2020 amid a drive to boost the volume it sells to China to 40% of its total sales, the company's chief executive said on Tuesday. (Reporting by Dave Sherwood)
