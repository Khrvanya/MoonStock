China’s Steel PMI Over 50, First Time Since SeptemberAccording to data released by China Steel Logistics Professional Committee of CFLP, the purchasing manager's index of the Chinese steel sector rose 8.4 percentage points from a month earlier to 52.6% in April, the first reading above 50% since September last year. Both new orders and new export orders indexes were above the 50% level and purchasing price index saw a big rebound from low level, showing signs of a recovery in China's steel market which remained in recession for a long period of time.
