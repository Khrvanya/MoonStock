Chinese Steel Exports Hit Record High For First 7 Months of 2014Bloomberg reported that China's exports of steel products during the first seven months of the year have surged to a record as domestic consumption falls amid increasing demand from the US and Europe.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering Steel/Iron Ore markets.According data released by China's General Administration of Customs, the world's biggest producer exported a record 49.1 million tons of steel products from January through July, an increase of 37% from the same period last year. Exports in July were 8.06 million tons, a 14% increase from the previous month and near the record in May.
