Commerce Rules for GOES Producers AK Steel, Allegheny; Monthly Price Index FallsThe Dept. of Commerce said Tuesday that GOES imports from Germany, Japan, Poland, Russia, and South Korea had arrived to the US at less than fair market value, e.g. "dumped." Duties in the range of 5.34% against South Korean producers to 241.9% from some German producers will go into effect immediately.
