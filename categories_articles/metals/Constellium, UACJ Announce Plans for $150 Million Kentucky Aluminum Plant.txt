Constellium, UACJ Announce Plans for $150 Million Kentucky Aluminum PlantConstellium N.V., a European multinational, and Japan's UACJ Corp. will build a $150 million aluminum production facility in Bowling Green's Kentucky Transpark, creating 80 jobs, the Park City (Ky.) Daily News reported today. The announcement follows a joint venture agreement between the two companies to supply aluminum Body-in-White sheet to the North American automotive industry.
