Copper Falls on the LME, Faces Gloomy OutlookConcern over weakening demand for copper, eased, somewhat after an economic growth report in China, the world's largest consumer, met expectations. China's 7.4 percent advance in gross domestic product was a little better than the 7.3 percent median estimate in a Bloomberg News survey of analysts but still down from the 7.7% rate of growth in the last quarter of 2013.
