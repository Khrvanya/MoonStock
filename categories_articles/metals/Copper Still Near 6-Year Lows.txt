Copper Still Near 6-Year LowsThere have been rough times in the commodity markets, but copper has been hit especially hard. From an early-2011 peak that approached $10,000 per metric ton, copper lost nearly 30% of its value in mid-2011, and after a brief recovery, has slowly dropped to its current price. Also on the LME, the copper 3-month price increased 1.4% to $5,665 per metric ton.
