Demand for Aluminum Down in January, Still Rises on LMEJanuary aluminum demand in the US and Canada - measured as shipments by domestic producers plus imports - totaled an estimated 2.018 billion lbs., down 2.9% from January 2013. On the LME, the aluminum 3-month price gained 0.2 percent to finish at $1,719 per metric ton. At $1,677, the cash price of primary aluminum finished the market day on the LME up 0.2 percent per metric ton.
