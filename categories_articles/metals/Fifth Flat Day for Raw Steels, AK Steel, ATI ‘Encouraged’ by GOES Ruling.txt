Fifth Flat Day for Raw Steels, AK Steel, ATI ‘Encouraged’ by GOES RulingThe\Department of Commerce recently announced import duties for shipments of grain-oriented electrical steel ("GOES") from China, the Czech Republic, Germany, Japan, Poland, Russia, and South Korea. The shipments were being sold at less than fair value (or "dumped"). AK Steel CEO James Wainscott and Allegheny Technologies CEO Richard Harshman, both domestic GOES manufacturers, released a statement saying they were "encouraged" by the new duties.
