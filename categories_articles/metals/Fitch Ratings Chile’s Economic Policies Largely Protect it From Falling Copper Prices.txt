Fitch Ratings Chile’s Economic Policies Largely Protect it From Falling Copper PricesChile's robust, flexible fiscal and macroeconomic policy frameworks are an important buffer against falling copper prices, Fitch Ratings says. Low public debt and significant resources in its stabilization funds also underpin shock-absorption capacity.
