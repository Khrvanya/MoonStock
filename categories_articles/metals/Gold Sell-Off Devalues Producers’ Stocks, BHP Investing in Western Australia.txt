Gold Sell-Off Devalues Producers’ Stocks, BHP Investing in Western AustraliaGold miners saw their stock values plummet with the price of the yellow metal on Monday. BHP Billiton is investing $240 million in its Western Australia iron ore tug boat and port business.The steep sell-off in shares of gold miners, tracking a plunge in the metal's price, wiped out more than $8 billion from their combined market value on Monday and pushed a global index of gold stocks to a six-and-a-half-year-year low.Free Download: July Metal Price ForecastThe Thomson Reuters Global Gold index slumped 8.5%  to its lowest since late 2008, the biggest one-day percentage drop in two years, after gold prices sank.BHP Billiton said today it will spend $240 million upgrading its marine iron ore facilities in Western Australia.
