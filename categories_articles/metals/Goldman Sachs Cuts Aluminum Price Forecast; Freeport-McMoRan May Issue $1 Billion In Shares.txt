Goldman Sachs Cuts Aluminum Price Forecast; Freeport-McMoRan May Issue $1 Billion In SharesGoldman Sachs Group has revised its outlook for the aluminum market and Freeport-McMoRan is considering issuing more than $1 billion in common shares to shore up its mining business.Goldman Sachs on Friday revised lower its aluminum price forecasts on expectations of a surplus in output between 2016 and 2019.
