Great Lakes Iron Ore Shipping Decreased 43% in March Due to IceIron ore shipping was significantly curtailed due to ice formations across the Great Lakes in March. Iron ore shipments in March were limited to 1.1 million tons, according to the Lake Carriers' Association based in Cleveland. That was a decrease of 43 percent compared to last year.
