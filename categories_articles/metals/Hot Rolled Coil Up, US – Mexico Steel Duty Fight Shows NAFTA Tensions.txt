Hot Rolled Coil Up, US – Mexico Steel Duty Fight Shows NAFTA TensionsMoves by US steel producers to seek anti-dumping duties against Mexican imports have led to a flare-up in trade tensions between neighbors in recent weeks ahead of key rulings on the cases in Washington.
