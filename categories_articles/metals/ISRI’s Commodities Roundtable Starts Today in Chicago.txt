ISRI’s Commodities Roundtable Starts Today in ChicagoThe Commodities Roundtable is the Institute of Scrap Recycling Industries' premiere event for buyers and sellers of scrap commodities. It includes roundtable discussions on copper, aluminum, ferrous metals, nickel/stainless and plastics. Each Roundtable takes an in-depth look at the commodity and provides important information recyclers can put to use the moment they get back to their office.The conference will be held today through Friday at the Hilton Chicago.
