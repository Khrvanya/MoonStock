Indian Steel Prices May Fall Further, HRC DownRecent appreciation of the Indian Rupee and falling international steel prices have forced steelmakers in that country to marginally cut prices of flat product like hot-rolled coil (HRC) in order to stay competitive with cheaper imports. The price-cutters, including Essar and JSW, however, are planning to increase prices of long products, especially TMT (themo mechanical treatment) bars as its demand continues to be good.
