Lundin Mining Buys Chilean Copper Mine From Freeport-McMoRanCanada's Lundin Mining Corp. has agreed to pay Freeport-McMoRan Inc. $1.8 billion in cash for an 80% stake in Chile's Candelaria copper operations to expand into the world's largest copper mining nation.
