Miners’ Strike, Russian Sanctions Factor Into 5% Drop for PlatinumThe price of platinum and its metal cousin, palladium, continued to see their prices move chaotically on the LME as the South African Miners' strike drags on and new sanctions against Russian individuals further threaten to curtail supplies. Russia is the second-largest producer of platinum in the world. South Africa is the largest and disruptions in the two countries that together produce most of the world's platinum could lead to additional costs for automakers if the metal becomes scarce.
