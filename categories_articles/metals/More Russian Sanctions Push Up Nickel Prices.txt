More Russian Sanctions Push Up Nickel PricesNew sanctions against Russian individuals and the renewed specter of sanctions against Russian industry are pushing nickel prices up and they have continued to climb for two days now. Russia's Norilsk Nickel is the world's largest producer of the metal after Indonesia began enforcing an export ban in January. Demand is still strong for the alloying metal in stainless steel.
