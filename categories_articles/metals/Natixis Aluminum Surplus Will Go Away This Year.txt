Natixis Aluminum Surplus Will Go Away This YearAluminum production has exceeded demand since 2007, but that surplus is likely to evaporate this year, according to a report from French financial-services firm Natixis. "In Russia and the West, producers continue to curtail unprofitable smelting capacity," the report states.
