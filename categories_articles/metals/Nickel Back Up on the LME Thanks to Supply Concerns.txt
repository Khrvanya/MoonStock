Nickel Back Up on the LME Thanks to Supply ConcernsNickel was back to its big gains on the LME yesterday as tensions over Ukraine threaten to push the alloying metal in stainless steel toward $19,000 per metric ton. Nickel prices have risen 34% this year mostly due to the Russia tensions, that country's Norilsk Nickel is the world's largest manufacturer, and an export ban in Indonesia that has dried up ore exports from the former biggest ore exporter.
