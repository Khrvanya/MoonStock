Nickel Price Drops as China Cuts Pig Iron OutputProducers of pig-iron nickel are expected to cut output, Reuters reported, as prices for ore imports have nearly doubled since the end of February in the wake of a ban on shipments by top exporter Indonesia. Reduced supply of nickel pig iron could mean China,  the world's top consumer of nickel and stainless steel, would increase its use of the higher grade refined metal, underpinning global prices that have already risen about 16 percent this year.
