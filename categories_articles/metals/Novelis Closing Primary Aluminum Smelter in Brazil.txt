Novelis Closing Primary Aluminum Smelter in BrazilNovelis has announced plans to shutter its Ouro Preto, Minas Gerais, Brazil, primary aluminum smelter by the end of 2014. Novelis says it is looking to focus its efforts in Brazil on its aluminum rolling and recycling operations.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering the Aluminum market."The decision to end operations at Ouro Preto is consistent with our global strategy to build our business around sustainability - transforming from a linear business model to a closed-loop business model focused on recycled content," Tadeu Nardocci, senior vice president, Novelis and president, Novelis South America told Recycling Today.
