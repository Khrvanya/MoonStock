Novelis Opens World’s Largest Aluminum Recycling Plant in Germany Novelis has opened the world's largest aluminum recycling center. Located adjacent to the company's rolling mill in Nachterstedt, Germany, the $258 million (EUR200 million) recycling center will process up to 400,000 metric tons of aluminum scrap annually, turning it back into high-value aluminum ingots to feed the company's European manufacturing network.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering the Aluminum market.The cash price of primary aluminum saw a 0.6% decline on the LME to $1,913 per metric ton.
