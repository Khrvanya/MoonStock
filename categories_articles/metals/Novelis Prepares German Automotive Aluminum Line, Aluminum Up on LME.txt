Novelis Prepares German Automotive Aluminum Line, Aluminum Up on LMENovelis recently broke ground for an $85 million automotive sheet finishing line at its Nachterstedt, Germany, facility. It will increase increase Novelis' annual automotive capacity by 120,000 metric tons as the company looks to capture a lucrative chunk of the automotive aluminum marketplace when 2015 corporate average fuel economy (CAFE) standards that demand lighter car and truck bodies kick in. Aluminum was up again today on the LME.
