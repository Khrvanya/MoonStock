Nucor Beats Estimates, Hot-Rolled Coil Futures Decline on LMENucor, the largest US steelmaker by market value, reported first-quarter earnings that beat analysts' estimates today. Net income rose to $111 million, or 35 cents a share, from $84.8 million, or 26 cents, a year earlier, the Charlotte, NC-based company said today in a statement. Profit excluding one-time items was 41 cents a share, beating the 38-cent average of 13 estimates compiled by Bloomberg News.
