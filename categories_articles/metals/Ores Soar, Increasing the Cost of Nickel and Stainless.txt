Ores Soar, Increasing the Cost of Nickel and StainlessIndonesia's ban on ore exports went into effect in January, yet stockpiles of nickel and steel ore kept markets from largely being affected until now. Nickel hit a one-year high Thursday as speculators bought up futures of the metal to bet on a shortage later in the year from the ban. China also recently said it would cut production of nickel pig iron as prices of the metal increased.
