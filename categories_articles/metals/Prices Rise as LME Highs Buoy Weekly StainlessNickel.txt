Prices Rise as LME Highs Buoy Weekly StainlessNickelNickel's strength showed no sign of slowing this week on the Stainless MMI. Chinese stainless manufacturers Baosteel and Taigang Stainless both raised prices in an attempt to keep up with nickel costs that have risen recently. The 3-month price of nickel on the LME closed on Friday at $15,480 per metric ton, recording a new nine-month high.
