Rare Earths MMI® Molycorp Nearly Doubles Production at Mountain PassMolycorp, Inc. reported that rare earth production in the fourth quarter of 2014 at its Mountain Pass, Calif., facility increased year-on-year and nearly doubled that of the preceding quarter.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering the Rare Earths market.Mountain Pass completed Q4 of 2014 with 1,328 metric tons  of rare earth oxide equivalent production. That compares to 1,034 mt in Q4 of 2013 and 691 mt in Q3 of 2014. Full year 2014 production totaled 4,785 mt, compared to 3,473 mt in 2013.This was welcome good news for the rare earths miner after it hired law firm Jones Day earlier this year for restructuring advice.
