Russian cenbank says Q1 economic growth seen at 2.0-2.5%MOSCOW, Feb 17 (Reuters) - Russia's central bank said on Monday it expected economic growth to stand at 2.0%-2.5% in the first quarter of this year. Earlier this month the bank had said it expected a moderate acceleration in economic growth in the first half of 2020. The central bank slashed the key rate for the sixth consecutive time this month amid slowing inflation, which as of January stood at 2.4%. Reporting by Elena Fabrichnaya
