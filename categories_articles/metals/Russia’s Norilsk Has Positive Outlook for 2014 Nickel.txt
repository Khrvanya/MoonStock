Russia’s Norilsk Has Positive Outlook for 2014 NickelNorilsk Nickel, the world's biggest producer of nickel and palladium, sees nickel prices recovering this year, it said on Monday after reporting a 64 percent drop in net profits due to write-offs, Reuters reported. Nickel prices are up 16 percent this year after large-scale producer Indonesia instituted an export ban of the alloying metal for stainless steel in January.
