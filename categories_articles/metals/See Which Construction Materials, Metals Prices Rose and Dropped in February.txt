See Which Construction Materials, Metals Prices Rose and Dropped in FebruaryThe biggest monthly gain in petroleum prices in more than 3 years caused construction materials prices to increase 0.4% in February, ending a six-month streak of falling prices, according to the March 13 producer price index release by the Bureau of Labor Statistics analyzed by the Associated Builders and Contractors of America.Free Download: Cut Your Shipping CostsYear-over-year construction input prices fell 3.9%.
