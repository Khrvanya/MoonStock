Silicon Rises 1.1%; First Solar Attempts to Halve Costs by 2018Silicon was the biggest mover on the weekly Renewables MMI(r) as First Solar, the world's top photovoltaic solar panel manufacturer, said this week that it expects its average manufacturing cost to nearly halve - from an average $US0.63/watt in 2013, to $US0.35/W in 2018.
