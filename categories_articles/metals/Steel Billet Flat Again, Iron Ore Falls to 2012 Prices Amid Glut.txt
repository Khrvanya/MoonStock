Steel Billet Flat Again, Iron Ore Falls to 2012 Prices Amid GlutIron ore retreated to the lowest level since 2012, capping a fourth weekly loss and nearing $100 a ton, as increased seaborne supplies of the steel-making raw material boosted a global glut, Bloomberg News reported. Ore with 62 percent content delivered to the Chinese port of Tianjin fell 1 percent to $102.70 a dry ton, the lowest level since September 2012, according to data from The Steel Index Ltd.
