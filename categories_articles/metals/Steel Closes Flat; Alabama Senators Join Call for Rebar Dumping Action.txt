Steel Closes Flat; Alabama Senators Join Call for Rebar Dumping ActionAlabama US Senators Jeff Sessions and Richard Shelby, both republicans,  joined 29 other senators in signing a letter to Commerce Secretary Penny Pritzker that asked the Commerce Department to consider the interests of the US steel industry as it investigates dumping allegations of reinforced bar, or "rebar," from Turkey and Mexico.
