Steel Imports Into the US Up in JanuaryBased on preliminary Census Bureau data, the American Iron and Steel Institute (AISI) recently reported that the U.S. imported a total of 2,577,000 net tons (nt) of steel in January 2016, including 2,217,000 nt of finished steel (up 10% and 8.8%, respectively, vs. December final data).Free Download: The February 2016 MMI ReportYear-to-date total and finished steel imports are 2,577,000 and 2,217,000 nt, respectively, down 41% and 38% respectively, vs. the same period in 2015. Finished steel import market share was an estimated 26% in January.Finished steel imports into the U.S. in January 2016.
