Tata Steel Reports $888 Million Loss, ABI Slips in AprilAn Indian steel major reports a loss and architecture billings slip in April.The Architecture Billings Index (ABI) dropped in April for the second month this year. As an economic indicator of construction activity, the ABI reflects a nine to 12 month lead time between architecture billings and construction spending.Pool 4 Tool's Automotive SRM SummitThe American Institute of Architects (AIA) reported the April ABI score was 48.8, down sharply from a mark of 51.7 in March.
