The Nickel Rally of 2014 Continues, Goldman Sachs Ups ForecastsGoldman Sachs Group has upped its three- and six-month nickel price forecasts due to the Indonesian export ban, Platts reported Wednesday. Goldman's three-month forecast now sits at $18,000 per metric ton (MT), up from $14,500, while its six-month forecast comes to $20,000 per MT, up from $15,000. Goldman has left its 12-month forecast at $16,000 per MT.
