U.S. Steel Idles Another Oil and Gas Tubular Steel Plant, 3-Month HRC FallsU.S. Steel has idled another oil country tubular goods (OCTG) steel plant in Houston, where 142 will be laid off. The facility generates more than 100,000 tons annually of steel pipes and tubes for oil and gas exploration and drilling.
