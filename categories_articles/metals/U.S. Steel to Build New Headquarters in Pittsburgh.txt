U.S. Steel to Build New Headquarters in PittsburghPNC Bank and even the Pittsburgh Penguins NHL hockey club did some major wheeling and dealing to keep U.S. Steel's headquarters in Pittsburgh.FREE Download: The Monthly MMI(r) Report - covering Steel/Iron Ore markets.The Penguins and developer Clayco of St. Louis will build, for U.S. Steel, a 5-story, 268,000-square-foot corporate headquarters on the former Civic Arena site by September 2017. As part of the deal, the company will receive New Markets tax credits and Local Economic Revitalization Tax Assistance (LERTA) tax abatements. The project potentially could qualify for a maximum of $7.5 million in property tax abatements over 10 years, depending on what is approved by the city, Allegheny County and Pittsburgh schools.
