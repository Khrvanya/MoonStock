U.S. stock funds lose $2.6 billion in week Lipper(Reuters) - U.S. equity funds saw outflows of $2.6 billion in the latest week, according to Lipper. U.S.-based taxable bond funds attracted $7.9 billion in the week ended on Wednesday, the seventh straight weekly inflow, and money market funds gained $6.3 billion, Lipper data showed.
