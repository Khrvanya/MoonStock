US Manufacturing and the EPA Clean Power PlanRules and regulatory compliance already got ya down? Well, another rule's comin'...(check out the video above for a preview.)The final rule of EPA's Clean Power Plan, set to go into effect mid-summer 2015, will likely have significant financial implications for US manufacturers.
