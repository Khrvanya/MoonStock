US Steel Increases PricesUS Steel announced, Monday, a base price increase on all new flat-rolled steel orders. The $25 per ton increase ($1.25/cwt) was to be "...applicable to all open quotations and/or negotiations where an agreement is not yet concluded...." All published pricing extras were to be applied to any new orders.
