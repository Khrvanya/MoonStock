US Steel Shows First Quarterly Profit in 18 MonthsUS Steel Corp. on Tuesday reported its first quarterly net profit in 18 months, thanks to cuts in costs and higher prices. The 113-year-old company notched a first-quarter profit of $52 million, or 34 cents a share, compared with a loss of $73 million, or 51 cents a share, a year earlier.
