Ukraine-Russia Conflict Pushes up Steel Billet Price at Black Sea PortSuppliers and buyers made attempts to outbid each other for steel billet at FOB Black Sea yesterday. Suppliers taking cue from shortage from Ukrainian mills owing to political crisis with Russia.
