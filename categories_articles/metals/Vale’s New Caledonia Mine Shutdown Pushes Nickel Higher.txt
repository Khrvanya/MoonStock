Vale’s New Caledonia Mine Shutdown Pushes Nickel HigherNickel rose on the LME Thursday as global supply concerns mounted following a plant closure in New Caledonia by Vale SA, the world's second-biggest producer. In the South Pacific nation, processing operations stopped after a spill of "acid-containing solution," a small amount of which entered a nearby creek, Cory McPhee, a Vale spokesman in Toronto, said today in an e-mail to Bloomberg News. The water quality in the creek is back to normal and Vale expects to "resume production ''shortly,'' he said, without citing a date.
